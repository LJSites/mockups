The Shop

Meet Your Mechanic

01

Midtown Automotive started with a simple idea: treat every customer's vehicle like it's the only one that matters. Chris is the kind of mechanic who reaches out when he can get you in sooner, stays after hours to finish the job, and tells you straight what does — and doesn't — need fixing.

From a tricky alternator on an '08 Lexus that another shop turned away, to full electrical and battery diagnostics, the work gets done with the kind of communication you rarely find under a hood. Set an appointment online, text questions anytime, and pay the easy way.

And because real life doesn't keep banker's hours, the door's open seven days a week.

— Chris · Owner & Lead Mechanic
