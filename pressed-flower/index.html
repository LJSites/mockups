The Boutique

A pressed-flower kind
of corner.

Pressed + Flower is a downtown Azusa coffee boutique built like a herbarium — pressed-botanical prints on the walls, bookshelves you can read from or take from, and small, thoughtful pieces tucked into every nook. It is the sort of room where you mean to stay twenty minutes and lose an afternoon.

Our baristas pour with care — from the red velvet latte with its painted foam to a honey-cinnamon cup or a whisked matcha. Pastries come out warm, the music stays soft, and there is always a window seat and a kid-friendly corner.

What you'll find
  • ✿ Handcrafted lattes & whisked matcha, with foam art on the house
  • ✿ Fresh pastries, croissants & danishes baked for the morning rush
  • ✿ Pressed-botanical décor, books to borrow, and a curated merch shelf
  • ✿ Communal tables, window seats & a quiet, library-calm room
  • ✿ A kid-friendly nook and free Wi-Fi for the long-stay crowd
