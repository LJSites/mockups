A mom-and-pop
patisserie.

The kind of bakery people drive across town for — and then quietly keep to themselves.

Bakery 101 blends French and Japanese pastry traditions: soft, fluffy chiffon layered with real whipped cream and fresh berries, cocoa-intense yule logs at the holidays, and a case of mousse cakes that look as good as they taste. Cakes are made to order, packed with care, and balanced so the flavor — not the sugar — comes through.

There's a patio out front for a coffee and a treat, and a freshly baked something often waiting if the line runs a little long. Come say hello.

02

What we bake

i

Whole Cakes & Slices

Berry-topped chiffon, chocolate whipped, black forest, and seasonal mousse cakes — available as whole cakes or by the slice, with a wider spread on weekends. Showpiece cakes for weddings, quinceañeras, and birthdays made to order.

ii

Signature Bûche de Noël

Our holiday tradition: a moist, cocoa-intense yule log that holds beautifully chilled or at room temperature. Order ahead during the season.

iii

Pastries & Croissants

Flaky cream-filled croissants, fruit pastries, and a rotating case of individual mousse and soufflé cakes — including a raspberry-and-chocolate soufflé that regulars come back for.

iv

Coffee Bar

Americano, caffè latte, and cappuccino to enjoy on the patio alongside whatever caught your eye in the case.

Specialty cakes — triple-berry chiffon, tiered celebration cakes — are best ordered a few days ahead. Give us a call and we'll take care of the rest.
