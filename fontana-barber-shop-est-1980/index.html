Our Story

Old-School Craft.
New-School Welcome.

Fontana Barber Shop has been a fixture on Foothill Blvd since 1980 — built on the simple belief that a great haircut deserves real attention, not a rush job. We specialize in all hair types, from tight coils to fine straight hair, and we take the time to do it right.

Our team — including our highly regarded lady barber — brings both speed and skill to every chair. Whether you're a long-time regular or stepping in for the first time, expect warm conversation, fair prices, and a cut that actually fits you.
