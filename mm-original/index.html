Our Story

A delightful surprise, every single time

MM Original started as a labor of love — a place where handmade pastries share shelf space with carefully chosen gifts, and where the staff remembers your name. Located on S. Glendale Ave, it's the kind of spot you stumble into once and keep coming back to.

Every baked good is made in-house. Every gift is picked with intention. The atmosphere is warm, unhurried, and genuinely inviting — exactly what this neighborhood deserves.
