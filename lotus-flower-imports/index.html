The Shop

Beautifully set up, and quietly tended.

Lotus Flower Imports has been a fixture of downtown Chico for years — a calm room where every dish, shelf and windowsill is given over to stone. Visitors tend to describe the same thing: a high, gentle vibe, and an owner happy to take all the questions you've got.

The selection runs from inexpensive tumbled pieces you can fill a pocket with, up to standing amethyst cathedrals and rare specimens you won't find elsewhere in the north state. Nothing is rushed; you're welcome to take your time and choose by hand.

Prices stay fair and the knowledge runs deep — which is exactly why people drive in from across the region and keep coming back.

The Collection

Not a catalogue — a room that rewards browsing. A sense of what waits on the shelves.

Bowls of tumbled stones sorted by color and kind, easy to gather a handful of and easier still to fall for. Standing amethyst & citrine geodes — from palm-sized to cathedral-tall — caught in the window light. Polished spheres, points and palm stones of rose quartz, fluorite and selenite. · ❧ · Trays of raw mineral specimens — pyrite, indigo gabbro, smoky and amethyst clusters — for collectors after the uncommon. And, always, a patient hand to tell you what you're holding and where it came from. Come in, take your time, and choose what speaks to you.
