Our Story

Flowers
Worth the
Early Rise

Every week, Erica is up at 2AM to make the drive to the Los Angeles flower market — hand-picking the freshest blooms so her clients never settle for anything less than exceptional.

Based in Tulare, we bring big-city flower quality to the Central Valley. Whether it's a birthday bouquet, a wedding crown, a sympathy arrangement, or a same-day delivery surprise — our shop delivers with care, speed, and a personal touch.

We speak Spanish and serve every community in our region. Walk in, call ahead, or ask about delivery. There's a bouquet here for every story.

Custom Bouquets

Hand-arranged fresh flowers tailored to your vision, occasion, and budget. No two bouquets are identical — each one is made for you.

Balloon Bouquets

Vibrant balloon arrangements perfect for birthdays, celebrations, and surprises — delivered with the same care as our floral designs.

Flower Crowns

Wearable floral art for weddings, quinceañeras, funerals, and photo shoots. Rush orders accommodated — our team can deliver in as little as one hour.

Phone & Walk-In Orders
