specialty drinks & a record wall

More than a coffee run.

Roasted Threads sits a block off the Midtown Farmers Market, where the espresso machine, the clothing racks, and a wall built from real vinyl records share the same room. Order a drink, then dig through the threads and crates while you wait — most people come for one and leave with both.

Specialty matchas, the signature Cinnabear, and a rotating calendar of in-store events keep regulars coming back — before work, after the market, or just to hang.
