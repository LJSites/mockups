About Jennifer

She listens.
Then she creates.

Nails By Jennifer88 is a one-on-one nail studio in the heart of Parlier. Jennifer brings seven-plus years of experience, a genuinely light hand, and an artist's eye to every appointment.

Tell her your idea — a mood, a color, a picture on your phone — and she makes it happen. Custom colors, hand-painted details, intricate gel designs. No cookie-cutter sets. No surprises on fit or finish.

She works with professional-grade products, takes her time, and treats every client like a regular. That's how customers keep coming back for years.

Services

What Jennifer does best

Every service is done by Jennifer personally — no assistants, no rush. Call ahead to book your appointment.

  • 01.

    Custom Gel Sets

    Full sets shaped and finished to your preference — almond, square, coffin, or stiletto. Gel overlay or extensions, built to last.

  • 02.

    Hand-Painted Nail Art

    Freehand designs painted directly on the nail — florals, abstracts, characters, or anything from your inspo pic. No decal shortcuts.

  • 03.

    Nail Art Decals & Embellishments

    Designer decals, rhinestones, and foil accents applied with precision — tasteful or extravagant, your call.

  • 04.

    Custom Color Matching

    Can't find the right shade? Jennifer mixes custom colors to match an outfit, a flower, or a feeling. Exactly what you had in mind.

  • 05.

    Fills & Maintenance

    Gel fills and shape refreshes for existing sets. Keep your look sharp between full appointments.

  • 06.

    Nail Removal & Natural Care

    Safe, gentle removal that protects the natural nail — no prying, no damage. Followed by conditioning if needed.
