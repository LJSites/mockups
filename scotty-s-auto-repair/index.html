About the shop

Real diagnosis. Real talk. Real repair.

Scotty treats every vehicle like it's his own. He'll walk you through exactly what's wrong, give you an honest quote, and get it done — often the same day. No runaround, no upselling, no surprises on the bill.

Located on Rosedale Highway in Bakersfield, the shop handles everything from complex AC jobs and power steering repairs to full diagnostics. If Scotty's busy, you're still in good hands — the wait is worth it.
