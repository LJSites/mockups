About Us

The nicest laundromat in the desert.

Laundry Room on Palm Drive has been serving Desert Hot Springs with clean machines, fair prices, and a genuinely well-maintained space. Owner Andrew keeps the place running tight — personally available even after hours — and the attendants sweep and wipe down equipment throughout the day.

Whether you're a local with a week's worth of clothes or a traveler with rugs that need a real wash, we've got the capacity to handle it all in one visit.
