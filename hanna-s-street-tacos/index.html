Hello from Higuera St

Small spot.
Big flavor.

Hanna's is a little street-taco window on Higuera in downtown San Luis Obispo — the kind of place you find on a lunch walk and end up coming back to every week. Everything's made to order, the line moves quick, and the tacos do the talking.

We keep it simple: good tortillas, good fillings, fresh salsa, and a counter that runs Tuesday through Saturday. Grab a couple to go or post up nearby and eat them while they're hot.
