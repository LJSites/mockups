01The Shop

A different kind of mechanic.

Precision Automotive Center isn't built around volume — it's built around getting it right. When a problem isn't obvious, the team digs in, researches it properly, and comes back with an honest appraisal so you can decide what's worth doing. No padded invoices, no guesswork billed by the hour.

From a '60s classic to a modern diesel, a Toyota truck to a daily-driver Corolla, the work is careful and the turnaround is quick. Customers come back because Scott, Matt, and the crew treat each car like it matters — and tell you the truth about it.
