The Shop

Your spot on the avenue.

Barber Avenue is the kind of place you come back to. Regulars settle in, the barbers crack jokes between cuts, and somewhere there's a game on the TV. It's relaxed — but the work is serious. The guys here know what they do and they do it well.

No appointment, no fuss. Pull up, hang out a minute, and walk out with a clean fade, a sharp lineup, or whatever you came in for. We're open seven days a week, so there's always a chair waiting.
