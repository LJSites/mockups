About the Shop

Family Owned.
Community Trusted.

Joe's Auto Repair is a small, family-run mechanic shop at 469 Moffat Blvd in Manteca. Joe and his daughter Cheyenne have been turning wrenches in the Central Valley for over three decades — with one rule: treat every car like it belongs to family.

Joe is known for being straight to the point — he explains exactly what needs to be done and why, without the pressure tactics or inflated invoices you'd get at a chain shop. Cheyenne brings the same ethic plus the flexibility to get customers back on the road the same day when it counts.

When you bring your car here, you get a real diagnosis from someone who cares, at a fair price. That's why customers drive across town — and sometimes across state lines — to come back.

What We Do

Services

Oil Changes

Conventional and synthetic oil changes with a multi-point inspection. Fast, no-upsell service done right the first time.

Brake Service

Pads, rotors, calipers, and brake fluid flushes. Joe diagnoses the actual problem — not the most expensive one.

Engine Diagnostics

Check-engine lights decoded and explained in plain language. No jargon, no guesswork — a clear plan before any work begins.

Tune-Ups

Spark plugs, filters, belts, and timing — everything it takes to keep your engine running at peak efficiency.

Suspension & Steering

Shocks, struts, tie rods, and alignments. If your car pulls, bounces, or wanders, Joe will find it.

General Repair

From cooling system work to electrical issues — if it's broke, this shop has likely fixed it before. Bring the hard stuff.
