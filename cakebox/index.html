Our Story

Ten Years of
Sweet Craft

Cakebox has been baking joy into Riverside for over a decade. Owner Jenny brings passion, precision, and genuine hospitality to every order — whether you walk in for a cupcake or commission a full wedding cake.

We believe every celebration deserves something extraordinary. From intricate fondant sculptures to light, fruit-topped cream cakes, our creations are designed to impress — and to taste even better than they look.

No order is too last-minute, no request too specific. We thrive on the challenge of bringing your vision to life.
