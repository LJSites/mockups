Our Story

A neighborhood
bakery with roots.

Chapala Bakery & Cafe has been a beloved corner of Greenleaf Avenue in Whittier — the kind of place locals return to every morning for warm concha, crispy churros, and a friendly face behind the counter.

We bake fresh every day, keeping prices honest and accessible for the whole community. Whether you're grabbing a dozen conchas on your way to work or settling in with a coffee, you're always welcome here.

Open 7 days a week, 6 AM to 9 PM — so there's always time for something sweet.
