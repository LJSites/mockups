A little about us

Big-shop know-how, neighbor-next-door service.

Guerrero's Auto Repair has kept Patterson's cars, trucks, and work rigs on the road for years — the kind of place where the person turning the wrench is the same person who explains what's going on, in plain English.

From AC recharges and check-engine diagnostics to four-wheel alignments and tire work, we handle the everyday stuff and the head-scratchers. No upsell games — just the repair your vehicle actually needs.

Pull into the lot on S El Circulo, and you'll usually have your answer the same day.

What we do

Everyday auto care, done right the first time.

One shop for the maintenance you can plan and the repairs you can't. Not sure what's wrong? Bring it in and we'll find out.

A/C Service & Recharge

R-134a and R-1234yf systems diagnosed and recharged on our Snap-on station — cold air for the Central Valley heat.

Wheel Alignments

Precision four-wheel alignments on our Hunter rack so your tires last and your steering tracks straight.

Tires & Rotation

Mounting, balancing, rotations, and flat repairs to keep you safe and rolling smooth.

Brakes & Suspension

Pads, rotors, shocks, and struts — the parts that stop you and keep the ride right.

Check-Engine Diagnostics

Code scans and real troubleshooting to find the actual cause — not just clear the light.

Maintenance & More

Oil changes, batteries, belts, fluids and the routine work that keeps small problems small.
