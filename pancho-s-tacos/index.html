Our Story

Made Fresh,
Served Warm

Pancho's Tacos is a neighborhood staple on Artesia Blvd in Torrance — the kind of spot where the refried beans remind you of home and the staff remembers your order. Everything is made to order and served the way it should be: warm, generous, and honest.

From loaded nachos and asada fries bursting with flavor to enchilada plates and burritos that go above and beyond, we put care into every plate. Drop in, pull up a seat, and taste the difference.

✓  Open 7 days — 7 AM to 8 PM

What We Serve

Fresh off the Line

Classic Mexican plates made daily. Generous portions, real ingredients, honest prices.

Asada Fries
Crispy fries loaded with carne asada, pickled jalapeños, carrots, and house sauces — a flavor bomb in every bite.
Burritos
Packed, warm, and wrapped tight. Guac, rice, beans — ask for your build and we'll nail it, even putting guac on the side if you need it.
Enchilada Plate
Enchiladas served with rice and beans, carrots, jalapeños, lime, sour cream, and a house garlic aioli that goes beautifully on the rice.
Nachos
Fresh and warm with authentic flavor. House salsa on the side — so good our guests ask for extra every time.
Tacos
Street-style tacos with the right toppings — the kind that remind you of the real thing, wherever home was.
Refried Beans
Our beans are the benchmark. If the beans are right, everything else is right. We take them seriously.
Chips & Salsa
House-made salsa that'll have you asking for a third bowl. Pickled jalapeños and carrots served alongside for the full experience.
Combo Plates
Mix and match your favorites with rice, beans, and all the fixings. Generous portions at prices that make sense.
