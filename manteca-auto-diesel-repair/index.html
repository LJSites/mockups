// The shop

Knows trucks. Looks out for you.

When a diesel starts running rough miles from home, you want the shop that actually wants to figure it out — not just throw parts at it.

Manteca Auto & Diesel Repair handles light- and heavy-duty trucks, classic builds, fleet vehicles, and the diesels other shops shy away from. Fuel filters and injectors, engine work, drivability and electrical diagnostics, brakes, and the smog-readiness runaround on older trucks.

Mark and the crew take the time to explain your options and find the cost-effective fix — the kind of straight, knowledgeable service that gets travelers and locals alike back on the road and recommending the shop after.
