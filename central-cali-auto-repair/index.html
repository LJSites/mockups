About the Shop

A neighborhood shop that treats you like family

Luis and the team at Central Cali Auto Repair have been keeping Patterson's vehicles running strong. Whether it's a quick A/C recharge or a complex engine repair, you'll get straight answers, fair prices, and work that lasts.

We're a small, hands-on shop — not a franchise, not a dealership. When you call, a real person picks up. When you drop off your car, the same mechanic who diagnoses it fixes it.

What We Do

Full-service auto care,
no surprises on the bill

A/C Service & Repair

Leak diagnosis, valve replacement, and full refrigerant service. We charge fairly — by the job, not by the ounce.

Engine Diagnostics

Check-engine lights, rough idle, unusual noises — we dig into the root cause before recommending any repair.

Brake Service

Pad replacement, rotor resurfacing or replacement, brake fluid flush. We won't sell you parts you don't need.

Door & Body Components

Latches, hinges, handles, and interior mechanisms repaired or replaced with precision and care.

Oil Change & Fluid Service

Conventional or synthetic oil, filter swap, and a full under-hood fluid check while you're in.

Towing & Emergency

Need a tow? We work with local carriers and can hold your vehicle securely while you arrange next steps.
