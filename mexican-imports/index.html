More than a snack shop

A whole mercado under one roof

Step into Mexican Imports and you'll find the flavors of home everywhere you look — bright bowls of fresh fruit, raspados in every flavor, and shelves stocked with the goods our Kerman neighbors grew up with.

Come for a mango-chile snack and stay for the Mexican candy, the hard-to-find medicine, the candles, and the everyday imports that make this place feel like a corner of Mexico right here in the Central Valley. And folks always mention one thing first: the staff are genuinely kind.
