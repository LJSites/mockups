A chill cantina where the plates come full.

Jalisco's Bar & Grill is the kind of place you look forward to every time you're in El Centro — friendly faces, a relaxed table you're never hurried away from, and food that tastes like somebody's family recipe.

From burritos that barely fit the plate to a clean, easygoing room made for a long afternoon, we keep it authentic, generous, and welcoming — pull up a chair and stay a while.
