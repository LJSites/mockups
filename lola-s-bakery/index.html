Our Story

A neighborhood bakery that bakes the way it always has.

Lola's has been a Chicago Avenue fixture for years — the kind of family bakery where the case is restocked all morning and the team will work with you on a cake right up to the day before your event. Bring in a photo of what you want; we'll make it happen.

From a dozen cupcakes for a gender reveal to a full wedding cake, the orders are affordable, the work is detailed, and the welcome is genuine. It's a real Riverside bakery, run by people who care how it tastes and how it looks.
