The Shop

A neighborhood donut counter, the way it should be.

SK's Donuts sits in a little storefront on South Coast Highway in Oceanside, with the bright red sign and the blue-and-white checkered floor. The owners are behind the counter, the cases are stocked before sunrise, and the line moves fast.

Regulars come for the classics — buttermilk, blueberry, apple fritters, maple bars, that signature cinnamon crumble — and stay for the big croissant breakfast sandwiches and smoothies. Bring the kids before school; you'll likely walk out with an extra donut hole or two.
