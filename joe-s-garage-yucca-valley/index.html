About the Shop

One-Stop Shop in the High Desert

Joe's Garage has been serving Yucca Valley and the surrounding High Desert community for years. Owner Joe and lead mechanic Blake built their reputation on honest diagnoses, fast turnarounds, and fair prices — the kind of shop you actually want to tell your friends about.

Whether you're a local coming in for routine maintenance or a visitor whose engine light just came on near Joshua Tree, we'll get you in quickly and back on the road with confidence. We work on everything from daily drivers to heavy F-350s, classic muscle cars, custom trailers — even rear-end swaps.

Joe and Blake love what they do, and it shows in every job. Quality welds. Clean installs. Work done down to a science.

What We Do

Services

01

General Repairs & Maintenance

Oil changes, brake service, tune-ups, spark plug replacement, belt and hose work — if it keeps your vehicle running, we handle it.

02

Engine Diagnostics

Check engine lights, sensor faults, drivability issues. We read the codes and find the real cause — not just clear the light.

03

Transmission Service & Rebuild

Full transmission rebuilds for cars and trucks. We rebuilt an F-350 transmission and had it back on the road in under a week.

04

Custom Fabrication & Welding

Frame work, custom beam installs, winch mounts, trailer tongue boxes — strong welds and clean custom metalwork.

05

Drivetrain & Axle Work

Rear-end swaps, differential service, axle repairs. We've done a full rear-end swap from a Ford Explorer into a '70 Dodge Dart.

06

Fleet & Repeat Clients

Regular customers bring us multiple vehicles — we track your fleet history and keep everything running at peak condition.
