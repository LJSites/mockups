Our Story

A Neighborhood Place Done Right

The Beerded Bean is a proudly independent coffee shop sitting on South Main Street in downtown Salinas. We sourced our beans with care, built out a space with real personality, and kept the prices honest — because a great cup of coffee shouldn't cost a small fortune.

"The coffee is great and reasonable priced. Perfect local spot to study or work."

Whether you're pulling in for your morning espresso, settling in for a long work session, or grabbing a pastry on your way through downtown, you'll find a warm welcome and something genuinely delicious waiting for you.

What We Serve

The Menu

Organic coffee, crafted drinks, and freshly made baked goods. Nothing fussy — just good ingredients treated with respect.

  1. Espresso & Classics

    Shots pulled to order — americano, cortado, macchiato, and a straight espresso that stands on its own.

  2. Lattes & Specialty Drinks

    Rotating seasonal lattes with house-made syrups, oat milk options, and barista-crafted originals you won't find at a chain.

  3. Cold Brew & Iced Coffee

    Smooth cold brew steeped slowly overnight — available straight, over ice, or blended. Our most-requested summer order.

  4. Organic Tea & Non-Coffee

    Loose-leaf teas, matcha, and steamed milk drinks for those who want warmth without the caffeine hit.

  5. Fresh Baked Goods

    Made locally and delivered fresh daily — muffins, scones, croissants, and a rotating selection of pastries that sell out early.

  6. Light Bites

    Grab-and-go sandwiches, toasts, and snacks that pair well with a long sit-down session at your laptop.
