Our Story

A Corner Table
for Generations

Some restaurants are destinations. Tommy's Family Restaurant is something rarer — it's a constant. Tucked on W Riggin St in Monterey Park, it has held the same corner and the same standard for so long that grandparents bring their grandchildren, and those grandchildren bring their kids.

The kitchen doesn't chase trends. It chases flavor — big albondigas in a broth that will actually fix what ails you, chile rellenos that earn the name, and tortillas that show up fresh and ready for whatever you have planned tomorrow.

Walk in feeling lousy, leave feeling taken care of. That's the whole idea.
