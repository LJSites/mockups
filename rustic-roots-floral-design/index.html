About Us

Jalisca's art — your celebration.

Rustic Roots Floral Design is the creative work of Jalisca, a floral artist based in the San Joaquin Valley who brings a signature style to every event — abundant, thoughtfully arranged, full of romantic elegance.

Whether you're planning a wedding, a birthday, or a quiet arrangement to brighten a room, each piece is crafted with the same love and attention: flowers chosen to tell a story, colors selected to set a mood, designs executed to stand apart.

Serving Exeter, Woodlake, and the surrounding Central Valley communities.

What We Offer

From bouquets to full wedding florals.

  1. Wedding & Event Florals

    Full-service floral design for weddings — bridal bouquets, ceremony arches, reception centerpieces, and more. Every detail coordinated to your vision.

  2. Custom Arrangements

    Hand-designed bouquets and arrangements for any occasion — birthdays, anniversaries, sympathy, or simply because. Fresh, seasonal flowers with a personal touch.

  3. Venue & Table Styling

    Tablescapes, altar pieces, aisle runners, and accent florals that transform any space. Designed to photograph beautifully and feel effortlessly elegant in person.

  4. Consultations & Custom Orders

    Every event is different — call or message to discuss your date, style, and budget. Jalisca works closely with clients to bring even the most particular vision to life.
