Who We Are

A Mom & Pop Shop
That Means It

Executive Image Cleaners is a true gem in Downtown LA — a classic, family-run operation where the owners know your name, remember your preferences, and treat every garment like it matters. Because to them, it does.

From delicate silks to everyday business suits, we've built a reputation for outstanding results and the kind of warm, personal service that a big city can rarely offer. Affordable pricing, meticulous attention to detail, and a genuine passion for the craft — that's the Executive Image difference.
