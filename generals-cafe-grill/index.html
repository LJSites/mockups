Our Story

A Delano Fixture, One Plate at a Time

Generals Cafe & Grill has been feeding Delano and its travelers for years — a warm, unpretentious spot where the ribeye-and-eggs breakfast is a rite of passage and the staff knows your name by the second visit.

Seven days a week, from first light through evening, we keep the grill hot and the coffee coming. Order off the menu or ask your server about the day's specials — here, the kitchen doesn't stop when the menu does.

Big portions, honest prices, and the kind of service that turns a first-time visitor into a regular. That's the Generals way.
