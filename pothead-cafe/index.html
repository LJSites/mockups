Our little corner

Tucked away, and worth finding.

Pothead Cafe is a charming, cozy coffee shop hidden in a small Mira Loma shopping center — the kind of spot regulars guard like a secret. Step inside and the personality hits you: warm, a little gothic, and unmistakably not a chain.

We pour drinks you won't find anywhere else — Oreo lattes, white chocolate lattes, dirty chais, Abuelita peppermint lattes, and shaken espressos people drive across town for. Every cup is made with care, by a crew that actually wants you to have a great morning.
