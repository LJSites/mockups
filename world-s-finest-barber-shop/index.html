The Shop

A welcoming chair, a steady hand, and a cut worth coming back for.

Tucked along Grapefruit Boulevard in Coachella, World's Finest is a full-service barber shop and salon where the whole family gets looked after. The room is clean, vibrant and easy to settle into — black walls, classic chairs, and a TV on while you wait.

Under new management, the shop now welcomes kids alongside the regulars, and keeps walk-ins and same-day chairs open all week. Whether it's a tight skin fade, a beard lineup or a fresh cut for the little ones, the goal is simple: send you out looking sharp at a fair price.
