The Shop

The mechanic who slides under your truck and tells you what you actually need.

George has been the kind of mechanic people drive across the desert to find. He won't upsell you a full exhaust system when a single muffler does the job. He won't guess — he diagnoses. And his front desk, run by Kaitlyn, keeps you informed from the first call to the last text.

You'll know what's wrong, what it costs, and when your car is ready — every time. The bill will be at or under the quote. That's been the promise here since day one.

Travelers coming through on I-10 know this, too. More than one person has driven off after a 20-minute fix that saved their road trip. That kind of reputation doesn't come from advertising — it comes from doing the job right.

What We Do

Services

General automotive repair and maintenance, from quick diagnostics to multi-day restorations. If it's got an engine, we can help.

Engine Diagnostics & Repair — check engine lights, electrical faults, rodent damage Air Conditioning Service — diagnosis, recharge, full component replacement Exhaust & Muffler — replace only what needs replacing Electrical Systems — wiring, sensors, computerized systems Brakes — pads, rotors, lines, fluid flush Oil Change & Fluid Service — conventional, synthetic, filter replacement General Maintenance — belts, hoses, tune-ups, inspections Roadside & Traveler Emergencies — same-day appointments when possible
