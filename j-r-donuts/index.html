Our Story

A neighborhood staple since day one

J-R Donuts has been the quiet pride of Bellevue Road for years. No frills, no fuss — just a wide, rotating variety of donuts made fresh every morning before the sun comes up. Regulars know to get here early; the glazed sell out first, and everything else follows by noon.

This is the kind of place where the owner knows your order and the coffee is always hot. A true Central Valley classic.

What We Make

Daily Specialties

Everything is made fresh before 5 AM. Variety changes daily — come early, come often.

01

Classic Glazed

Light, airy rings dipped in a signature glaze. The first to sell out — every single morning.

02

Cake Donuts

Dense, satisfying, and available plain or dipped. A longtime customer favorite.

03

Filled Donuts

Plump and generously filled — bavarian cream, jelly, and seasonal flavors when available.

04

Raised & Frosted

Fluffy yeast-raised shells topped with chocolate, strawberry, maple, or vanilla frosting.

05

Apple Fritters

Hand-shaped, generously sized, irregular and perfect — the way a real fritter should be.

06

Donut Holes & Twists

Bite-sized glazed holes and cinnamon twists — great by the bag, gone even faster.
