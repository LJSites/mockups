About Us

Your neighborhood barbershop on the 111

One Love Cutz Barbershop 2 is where great cuts meet an even better atmosphere. Our barbers bring professionalism, precision, and good energy to every chair — whether you're after a tight fade, a classic taper, or a lineup that has you looking sharp.

We've built a reputation in Indio for being the kind of shop where you leave better than when you walked in — not just your hair, but your whole mood. Come through, relax, and let us do the rest.
