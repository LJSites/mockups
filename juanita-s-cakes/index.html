Our Story

Baked with Heart for Delano

Juanita's Cakes has been feeding the families of Delano with fresh-baked goods made the right way. Every bolillo, telera, and custom cake comes out of our oven the same morning you pick it up.

We're a neighborhood bakery through and through — trusted by home cooks, restaurants, and taqueria owners who won't settle for anything less than the real thing. Come see us any day of the week.

Our Offerings

    Birthdays, quinceañeras, graduations — tell us the occasion and we'll bake something unforgettable. Decorated to order in classic and contemporary styles.
