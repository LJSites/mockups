The Shop

Behind every
bloom is Daisy.

Daisy Flowers is a neighborhood flower shop on Cherry Avenue in Long Beach — the kind where the owner knows your name and builds your arrangement right in front of you. Daisy runs it herself, and her warmth is as much a part of the product as the flowers.

Bring a budget, a color, and a reason — she handles the rest. Same-hour custom arrangements, consistent freshness, and prices that don't make you apologize for wanting something beautiful.
