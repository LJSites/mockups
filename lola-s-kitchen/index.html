Our Story

A real local spot — worth getting off the freeway for

Lola's Kitchen is a vibrant, casual eatery on Barstow's East Main, serving the traditional Mexican fare our neighbors (and a whole lot of road-trippers) keep coming back for. Hand-made plates, free chips & salsa while you wait, and the kind of friendly, lightning-fast service you only get from folks who actually cook the food.

It's only about a mile off the interstate — spend your money locally with good people, and get a far better meal for it.
