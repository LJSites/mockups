Hi, we're so glad you're here

A little flower shop with a big heart

Flower St Flowers is a family-run shop in the heart of Bellflower, run by a couple of very sweet ladies who love nothing more than helping you pick out the perfect arrangement.

Bring us a photo, an occasion, or just a feeling — and we'll build something even prettier than you imagined. We'll ask if you'd like a vase, a bow, a card, a little fragrance… the whole experience, with great music playing the whole time.

From a single rose to a full event, we've got fresh blooms, keepsake crochet flowers, and a price tag that won't break the bank.
