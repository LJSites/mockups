About the Shop

Your Car in Good Hands

Mechanics Express and Import Repair has been a trusted fixture at 201 D St in Patterson for families and travelers alike. Owner Erin (also known as Irwing) built this shop around a simple promise: show up when people need help, tell them the truth, and get them back on the road.

Whether you're a Patterson local in for routine maintenance or a traveler who broke down on the interstate, the doors are open Monday through Saturday. The team even drops customers at home when they leave their vehicles — the kind of small-town hospitality that's genuinely rare.
