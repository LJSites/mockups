Our Story

Cooked the way you'd cook for family.

Grand Kitchen is a family-run cafe tucked on South Adams Street, serving breakfast, lunch, and brunch to the Glendale neighborhood. We're a simple spot — counter service, a few tables — but the cooking is anything but ordinary.

You'll find lentil soup and yogurt soup, kale & quinoa salads, perogi, avocado toast, fluffy pancakes, kabob plates, and carefully made coffee. Anna and the team are happy to walk you through the menu, talk ingredients, and never make you feel rushed. Healthy options are hard to find around here — so we lead with them, and put care into every dish.

— Anna & the Grand Kitchen family
