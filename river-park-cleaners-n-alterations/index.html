Who you'll meet

The kind of shop you keep going back to.

River Park Cleaners n Alterations is a small, hands-on shop on E Vineyard Avenue in Oxnard. We press garments, fix what's torn, take in what's too loose, and let you knock out a Live Scan or a passport photo on the same visit.

People come back because we're easy to deal with and we do honest work — the nicest people doing a quality job, as our regulars put it. When you've got a last-minute alteration or a rush clean before something important, we'll do our best to make it happen.

If you're doing a Live Scan for the first time, ask — we'll explain every step so you walk out sure it was done right.
