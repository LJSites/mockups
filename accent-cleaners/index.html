About the shop

For more than three decades, Santa Ana has trusted Accent Cleaners with the clothes that matter most.

From the everyday shirt to the once-in-a-lifetime wedding gown, every piece is treated with the same patient, hands-on care. The team is known for rescuing garments others give up on — set-in stains, delicate fabrics, alterations that need to fit just right.

We use environmentally friendly cleaning processes and even recycle our hangers and plastics — a cleaner you can feel good about, inside and out.
