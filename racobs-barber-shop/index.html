The Shop

Old-school care, every single chair.

Step into Racobs and you'll feel it right away — the easy conversation, the buzz of the clippers, the kind of place where the barber remembers your name and your fade. Folks have come up through a lot of shops in the High Desert and keep landing here for one reason: the work is good and the welcome is real.

Our barbers take their time and take pride in the details — clean line-ups, blended fades, comfortable shaves, and beard work done right. We're patient with kids, including those who find the chair a little overwhelming, and we treat every head of hair with the same steady, careful hands.

No attitude, no rushing — just an honest cut at a fair price, in a shop that's been part of the neighborhood for years.
