NUESTRA HISTORIA

From a beloved truck to your new favorite table.

XX Tacos started turning heads serving the kind of street tacos people drive across town for. So when we opened the doors to our own spot on Watt Ave, the regulars showed up day one — and the food's only gotten better.

Bright, clean, and full of color, it's a sit-down taqueria with truck-stand flavor. Come for the quesabirria, stay for the strawberry-pineapple agua fresca, and meet the owners while you're at it.
