Our Story

A Cut You Don't Have to Explain Twice

Scully's has been an Apple Valley staple since the beginning. What sets us apart isn't a gimmick — it's that our barbers actually listen. They read what you want, ask the right questions, and deliver the cut you had in mind.

Three main barbers, consistent quality, and a welcoming shop atmosphere. Whether you're coming in for a quick tighten-up or a full haircut and beard trim, you'll leave looking sharp — every single time.

Walk-ins only. No appointments needed — just come on in. Cash only.
