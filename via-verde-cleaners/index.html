Our Story

The couple behind the counter knows your name.

Via Verde Cleaners has been a fixture on Via Verde for as long as San Dimas neighbors can remember. The husband-and-wife team who run it bring genuine warmth to every drop-off — regulars are greeted by name, garments are handled with real attention, and nothing leaves the shop until it's right.

From everyday dry cleaning and shirt pressing to intricate alterations for weddings, formals, and everything in between, they do the work themselves — and they stand behind it.
