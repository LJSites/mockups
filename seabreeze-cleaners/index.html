The shop on the corner

The kind of place
that remembers you.

Seabreeze Cleaners has looked after Oxnard's wardrobes from the same Harbor Boulevard storefront for years — close enough to the channel that the morning fog still rolls in off the water.

It's a small team that treats your laundry like it's their own. Customers follow Isabel from as far as Camarillo, trusting her with their families' garments year after year. When you walk in, you're greeted by name; when you've got a back too sore to lift the basket, someone carries it for you.

Honest pricing, careful pressing, and the quiet confidence of a place that's been doing this a long time.

What we look after

Drop it off in the morning, often back sooner than you'd expect. Everything below is handled in-house.

01

Dry Cleaning

Suits, dresses, delicates and everyday garments cleaned with care and returned looking sharp — no shortcuts, no surprises.

02

Laundry & Wash

Loads big and small, washed and folded. Bring the whole basket — we'll even help you carry it in from the car and back out again.

03

Pressing & Finishing

Crisp collars, clean creases and properly finished garments. Shirts that look the way they did the day you bought them.

04

Alterations

Hems, fit and repairs done right. A nicely-done alteration is the difference between a garment you tolerate and one you reach for.
