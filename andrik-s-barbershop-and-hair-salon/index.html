The Shop

Two Generations,
One Good Chair.

"A modern-day mother-daughter barbershop — fantastic cuts and designs, and just really nice folks."

Andrik's sits on the brick corner of West Walnut Avenue, where the barber pole in the window has become a small downtown landmark. Inside it's part barbershop, part salon: clippers buzzing on a fresh skin fade on one chair, color and a blow-out on the next.

The mother-daughter duo behind the shop have built their reputation one regular at a time — careful with the kids, generous with the conversation, and proud of every line they cut. Walk in for a quick trim or settle in for the full treatment.
