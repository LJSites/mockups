Our Stand

A real flower market, not a webpage that ships from a warehouse.

Walk into A Martinez Flowers and you'll find buckets of fresh-cut stems, walls of ready-made bouquets, and a tented stand out front stacked with roses by the case. We've grown into one of the friendliest flower stops in San Bernardino by doing one thing well: beautiful flowers at prices people can actually afford.

Tell Luis, Claudia or the team your budget and your occasion — they'll build something better than you pictured, often while you wait.
