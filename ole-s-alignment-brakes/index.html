About Ole's

Seasoned mechanics.
Straight answers.

Ole's Alignment & Brakes is a no-nonsense shop at the heart of Yucca Valley. The team here has spent decades working on the desert roads' demands — vehicles that take real punishment on rough terrain, long stretches of highway, and unforgiving heat.

What keeps customers coming back isn't just the quality of work — it's the honesty. When something doesn't need fixing, they'll say so. When they diagnose an issue, they explain it plainly. If you bring your own parts, they'll work with you on pricing. It's old-school shop integrity with experienced hands behind it.

What We Do

Core services

Precision work on the systems that keep you safe — alignment, brakes, and suspension — done by mechanics who've seen it all.

01 / Core

Wheel Alignment

Precise four-wheel alignment corrects your vehicle's angle to factory spec, preventing uneven tire wear and keeping your rig tracking true on the highway. We check toe, camber, and caster — then show you the before/after readout.

Most requested service
02

Brake Service

Brake pads, rotors, calipers, brake lines, and fluid — inspected and serviced as needed. We won't replace parts that still have life in them. Your safety is the metric, not our invoice.

Brakes & rotors
03

Suspension Repair

Worn shocks, struts, CV joints, tie rods, and control arms — suspension problems show up as noise on tight turns, pulling at highway speed, and rough ride quality. We diagnose and fix at the source.

Handling & ride quality
04

Diagnostic Inspection

Hearing a noise but not sure what it is? Bring it in. We put it on the lift, test drive it, and give you a straight answer — even if that answer is "nothing is wrong right now."

No-pressure evaluation
05

General Maintenance

From oil changes and tire rotations to fluid checks — the routine work that extends vehicle life. Bring your own parts and we'll apply our labor at a fair price.
