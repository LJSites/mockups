About Us

A shop that follows up.

Boreas Automotive is a full-service auto repair shop serving the Calexico community. Owner Hector brings deep mechanical experience and a hands-on approach — you'll actually talk to the person working on your vehicle.

We offer flexible financing options so you're never forced to choose between safety and your budget. When your car leaves our bay, we make sure it stays fixed — and we check in afterward to confirm it did.

What We Do

Full-service repair
you can count on.

From routine maintenance to complex engine diagnostics, Boreas handles it. We work on domestic and import vehicles — cars, trucks, SUVs, and Jeeps — with honest estimates and no upsell pressure.

  • Engine Diagnostics & Repair
    Check-engine codes decoded, cylinder head, cooling system — we get to the root cause.
  • Cooling System Service
    Overheating repairs, radiator flush, thermostat and water pump replacement.
  • Brakes & Suspension
    Brake pad/rotor replacement, caliper service, struts, shocks, and alignments.
  • Oil Change & Preventive Maintenance
    Factory-interval service, filters, belts, and fluid checks — keep it rolling smooth.
  • Transmission Service
    Fluid exchange, filter replacement, and transmission diagnostics for automatics and manuals.
  • Electrical & AC Systems
    Battery, alternator, starter, and full climate-control diagnosis and repair.
  • Financing Available
    Ask us about flexible payment options — repairs shouldn't wait.
