About the shop

A neighborhood mechanic people come back to for years.

Hans Auto has served drivers in the W Pico / Koreatown area through everyday oil changes, brakes and tune-ups all the way to cooling-system and transmission work. Longtime customers — many with cars well past 100,000 miles — keep their vehicles running here because the work is fast, fair, and straightforward.

The owner takes the time to explain what's going on and focuses on the repairs that matter. It's the kind of place where a small fix gets handled as a courtesy, and where you leave knowing exactly what was done to your car.
