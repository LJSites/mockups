About Us

Your Neighborhood Shop with a Decade of Loyal Customers

All Valley Transmission & Auto Repair has served the Clovis and greater Fresno community for years as a full-service shop specializing in transmissions and general mechanical work. We're a small team that takes the time to explain what's wrong, why it matters, and what fixing it actually costs — no inflated estimates, no upselling. Whether you've driven across town for us or were just referred by a friend, we work to earn that trust on every visit.
