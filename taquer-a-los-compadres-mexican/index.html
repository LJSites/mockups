Our Story

Compadres in Every Bite

In Red Bluff, where Antelope Boulevard meets the everyday rhythm of a working California town, Taquería Los Compadres has quietly built a reputation for the kind of food that stops you mid-conversation.

We're talking flautas you can't put down, super burritos sized for today and tomorrow's lunch, and carne asada tacos that regulars drive across Tehama County to claim as the best they've ever eaten.

The salsa carries real heat — the kind that complements, not overpowers. The portions are generous. The welcome is warm. That's Los Compadres.
