Our Story

Flowers Made
Personal

At Fantasy Flowers and Gifts, we work with your vision and your budget. Every arrangement is hand-crafted with fresh, quality blooms — and we're known for going the extra mile, adding touches at no extra charge because we genuinely care.

Monica, Maricely, and the whole team have built a reputation on warm, fast, and reliable service. Same-day delivery is our specialty — call in the morning, receive beauty by afternoon.

Serving Lynwood and the greater South LA area — proudly the Best Flower Shop in LA, as our customers say.
