About Us

A little store
with a lot of heart.

La Morena Market is a family-run convenience store in the heart of Sanger. We open before dawn so early risers — farmworkers, commuters, neighbors — always find fresh bread, cold drinks, and a friendly face waiting for them.

The owners pour genuine care into everything they stock. Customers keep coming back not just for the groceries, but for the feeling of community that comes with every visit.
