About the Shop

Hands-On Expertise, Not a Corporate Checklist

Damien and the D&J Auto team specialize in the vehicles most shops won't touch — vintage VWs, air-cooled classics, custom dune buggies, replica speedsters, and the kind of project cars that need someone who genuinely knows what they're doing.

Every build and repair gets a straight, honest assessment. If there's a deadline, they find a way. If there's a better approach, they'll walk you through it. No corporate scripts, no upsell pressure — just reliable work from people who love these cars as much as you do.
