The Salon

A clean, bright space & a team you'll trust.

Nail Fashions & Lashes is a full-service nail salon in Colton run by owner Phong — a name longtime regulars know by heart. From a fresh gel manicure to a relaxing spa pedicure or a crisp French set, every detail gets done right.
