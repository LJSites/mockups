Our Story

A hidden gem
worth finding

Tucked off the main drag in Delano, Tota's Cakery & Coffee Bar has become the neighborhood's quiet obsession. Come for a custom-designed cake, stay for a frappe that puts the chains to shame.

Every cake is made to order — moist layers, real frosting, and decorations that match the moment. The coffee bar serves house frappes and specialty drinks alongside fresh-baked cinnamon rolls, including the legendary Biscoff variety that regulars drive across town for.

The team treats every customer like family. As one regular puts it, "I've never been so welcomed into a place."
