A little about us

The corner cleaner that treats your clothes like its own.

Plaza Cleaners is a family-run dry cleaner and laundry tucked into the Lathrop plaza on Louise Avenue. From a favorite Pendleton board shirt to a suit you need first thing tomorrow, we handle every garment with care — and we'll tell you straight what something needs.

What keeps neighbors coming back isn't just clean clothes. It's the people. Folks like Patricia go out of their way to make things right, and we'd rather earn your trust than rush you out the door. Drop in and you'll see what we mean.
