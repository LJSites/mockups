Our second location

A market, a juice bar & a kitchen — under one roof.

Walk in and you'll find big jars of ruby watermelon, green pepino, and golden piña agua fresca lined up beside a wall of fresh fruit. Order a bionico, a Cinnamon Toast Crunch milkshake, a paleta, or sit down for all-day breakfast and lunch.

Antojitos Martin #2 is the newer Centinela spot — same energy, same friendly faces, same love for variety. Smoothies, ice cream, tortas, ceviche, tamales, wraps and more, all in one bright corner of Inglewood.
