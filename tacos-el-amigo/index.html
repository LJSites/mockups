Our Story

Real Ingredients,
Real Flavor

Tacos El Amigo has been serving Madera with the kind of homestyle Mexican cooking that keeps you coming back — burritos packed with the perfect ratio of rice, beans, guacamole, and your choice of meat, wrapped in chewy handmade tortillas.

From crispy flautas to freshly fried sopes and smoky asada tacos, every plate is made with care. Wash it down with house-made horchata, jamaica, or piña.
