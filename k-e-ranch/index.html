Our Story

A Ranch Shop Full of Good Things

K&E Ranch is a family-run shop nestled in the farmlands of Sanger. Owner Kim built this place around one idea: that wholesome, delicious food and thoughtful gifts shouldn't be hard to find. From gluten-free loaves to curated snack selections and seasonal gift baskets, every item here is chosen with love.

Gluten-free options available — because everyone deserves great bread
Gift baskets custom-assembled for families, friends & businesses
Open Mon–Sat, 10 AM to 6 PM — always something new on the shelf
