Meet your barber

Shane makes the chair feel easy.

At Off The Top, a haircut is more than a haircut — it's a few minutes of feeling welcome. Shane is the barber Oroville families ask for by name, known for steady hands, a kind word, and the patience to make even nervous first-timers comfortable.

Parents of kids with autism, sensory sensitivities, and special needs come from all over town because they know their child will be treated with care — high-fives, "I'm so proud of you!", and a cut that actually looks great.

Sharp fades for the grown-ups, gentle trims for the little ones, and a friendly salon chair for the whole family. Walk in a stranger, leave a regular.
