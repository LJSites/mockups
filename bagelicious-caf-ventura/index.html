Our Story

A Café That Cares About Its Community

Bagelicious Café Ventura sits on East Main Street where warm service is as important as a warm bagel. Owners Alma and Adam built this place on a simple idea: every customer who walks in should feel remembered, welcomed, and fed well.

From jalapeño bagels to classic lox on an everything, from freshly squeezed organic juice to smooth bold coffee — every item is made with intention. It's the kind of spot New Yorkers nod at and Ventura locals claim as their own.

— Alma & Adam

What We Make

The Menu

Everything baked fresh. Everything made to order.

  1. 01

    Hand-Crafted Bagels

    Baked in-house every morning — crispy outside, soft and chewy inside. Classic plain, sesame, everything, jalapeño, and more rotating daily.

  2. 02

    Breakfast Bagel Sandwiches

    Start with your choice of bagel, then layer on eggs, cheese, meats, and fresh toppings — including fan favorites like The Crazy Bagel.

  3. 03

    Lox & Cream Cheese Spreads

    New York-quality lox on a toasted everything bagel. House cream cheese spreads in a variety of flavors — full of freshness and flavor.

  4. 04

    Pastries

    Rotating selection of fresh-baked pastries made on the premises. Perfect alongside your morning coffee.

  5. 05

    Coffee & Fresh Juice

    Smooth, bold coffee brewed with care. Freshly squeezed organic orange juice — real fruit, no bottles, no shortcuts.
