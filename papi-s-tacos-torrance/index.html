Our Story

HANDMADE.
HONEST.
MEXICAN.

Papi's Tacos brings the bold flavors of Mexico to Torrance. We're a neighborhood taqueria serving birria with rich consommé, carne asada grilled fresh, and loaded nachos stacked the right way — generous, saucy, and worth the trip.

Located inside The Enclave on W 190th St, we've built a loyal following one taco plate at a time. Every dish is made with care and seasoned for the real thing.

What We Serve

THE MENU

Fan Favorite

BIRRIA TACOS

Slow-braised birria in hand-pressed tortillas, served with a side of rich, deeply spiced consommé for dipping. Order these.

Build Your Own

TACO PLATE

Pick your proteins — carne asada, chicken, fried fish, or veggie. Three tacos per plate, each one dressed and ready.

Must Try

LOADED NACHOS

Stacked chips topped with your choice of protein, cheese, jalapeños, and all the fixings. A meal on its own — bring a friend or don't, we don't judge.

Classic

ASADA QUESADILLA

Generous portion of grilled carne asada folded into a crispy flour tortilla with melted cheese. Big flavor, big size.
