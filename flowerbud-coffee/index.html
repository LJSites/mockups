Our Story

A hidden gem, grown with care.

Tucked off Rubidoux Boulevard, Flowerbud Coffee is the kind of place you stumble onto and never forget — beautiful inside and out, full of greenery, and quietly buzzing with good morning energy.

We pour thoughtful coffee and genuinely lovely matcha, from a tummy-friendly Spanish latte to bright, plant-based options. Whether you settle into the couch indoors or claim a table on the patio (yes, there's a Connect Four), we built this little space to feel like a garden you can actually relax in.
