MILE 01

A neighborhood shop with a road-trip reputation

Highway 33 Automotive sits right in town in Patterson — but ask the travelers who limped off I‑5 and they'll tell you it feels like the kind of garage you wish lived on your block.

Run by Jason and his crew, Highway 33 is the spot locals trust for the everyday stuff — oil and brakes, throttle bodies, check-engine diagnostics — and the place stranded drivers get towed to when their road trip goes sideways.

We tell you exactly what's wrong, what it costs, and what can wait. No guesswork, no upsell.

Cars, trucks, motorcycles, and the occasional 1975 Airstream motorhome — if it's got wheels and a story, we'll figure it out and get you moving again. Fair prices, straight answers, and a phone call the next day to make sure you made it.
