The Studio

A quiet little gem on March Lane.

T Care Nails & Spa is a newly opened, family-run studio where every set gets unhurried, careful attention. Walk in for an almond-shape cat-eye full set or settle in for a relaxing pedicure — the room stays calm, the space stays spotless, and the products are kept high-quality and clean.

Sophie and Tammy lead the chairs, shaping and painting with the kind of patience that turns a quick visit into the highlight of your week. Walk-ins are welcome, and there's rarely a wait.

“What a gem.” — and we intend to keep it that way.
