What We Do

Full-Service Auto Repair

From the straightforward to the head-scratchers other shops pass on — Taylor Automotive handles it.

Engine Diagnostics & Repair
Check-engine lights, hard-to-diagnose running issues, multi-shop problems that finally get solved here. Bob's team digs until they find the root cause.
Oil Changes & Fluid Service
Conventional and synthetic oil changes, coolant flushes, transmission fluid, and all routine fluid maintenance to keep your car running like it should.
Brake Inspection & Repair
Pads, rotors, calipers, and brake fluid — inspected and replaced with quality parts. You'll know exactly what's needed before anything gets done.
Suspension & Steering
Shocks, struts, tie rods, wheel bearings — if something feels off in how your vehicle handles, we'll find it and fix it right.
Electrical System
Batteries, alternators, starters, and wiring issues. Modern vehicles are wired for complexity — we have the tools and experience to work through it.
Pre-Purchase Inspections
Thinking about buying a used vehicle? Bring it in. We'll give you a full, unbiased assessment of what you're actually getting into.
