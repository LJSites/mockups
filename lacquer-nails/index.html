About

Lacquer Nails is a Stockton salon built on one idea: never treat a guest like a dollar sign.

Tucked into Lincoln Center, we’re an inviting, relaxing room where regulars come back season after season for the same technicians they trust — names like Kim, Julie, and Teri, who’ve built their reputations one careful set at a time.

Whether it’s a quick polish change or a full gel set, the goal is the same: take the time to get it right.
