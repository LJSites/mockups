Our Story

The corner flower shop Ramona trusts.

Gonzalez Flowers has been a fixture on Main Street for years — the kind of shop where you can walk in with a photo you loved on your phone and walk out with the real thing. Simon works with a huge selection of fresh blooms and builds each arrangement to order, whether it's a single dozen roses or a full wreath.

Reasonable prices, real flowers, and the flexibility to make exactly what you pictured — even painting white roses purple when that's what the moment called for.

— Simon, Gonzalez Flowers
