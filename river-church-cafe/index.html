The place

A neighborhood cafe, the way they used to be.

River Church cafe sits right on East Broadway, a few steps from the heart of downtown Anaheim. It's the kind of spot people fold into their week — the Sunday-morning ritual, the mid-afternoon reset, the catch-up that runs a little long because the seats are too comfortable to leave.

The brews are smooth, the pastries come out fresh, and the welcome is genuine. No rush, no fuss — just a warm, lively room and a cup made with care.
