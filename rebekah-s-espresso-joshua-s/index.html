Our Corner of Lemoore

The little espresso bar on Hanford Armona Road.

Rebekah's Espresso & Joshua's is a family-run coffee stop tucked into Lemoore, just off the Hanford Armona Road. Pull up to the drive-thru or step inside — either way you'll find drinks pulled with care and a barista who's genuinely glad you came by.

We're known around town for our dirty chai, frosted coffee coolers, and a rotating barista's daily pick scrawled on the board out front. Beans are roasted into our own blends — the Felleke, the Blue Nile, French Vanilla and Hazelnut — and you can take a bag home, too.

Don't be shy at the drive-thru: there's a window bell if no one's at the glass, since the same hands sometimes help next door. Ring it — your coffee's worth the moment.
