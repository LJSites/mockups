Our Story

A Hollister hidden gem, one super burrito at a time.

Tucked on Tres Pinos Road, Super Taquería has been the spot locals send out-of-towners to for years. The recipe is simple: fresh ingredients, a counter that greets you with a smile, and burritos hefty enough to make a dollar stretch.

Carnitas and carne asada are the headliners, but the tortas, flautas, quesadillas and aguas frescas keep the regulars coming back. Pull up a booth, grab a salsa bottle, and stay a while — it's that kind of place.
