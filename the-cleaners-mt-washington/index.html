The Cleaners has been taking care of Mt Washington's garments for years. Locals drive past closer options to come here — because once you've experienced the quality, you don't settle for less.

From everyday dry cleaning to delicate costume steaming and precise alterations, our team handles every garment with the care it deserves. Drop off before work; pick up on your way home.

What We Do

Our Services

Professional garment care for everyday essentials and one-of-a-kind pieces alike. Every item gets individual attention.

Dry Cleaning

Suits, dresses, coats, and delicates cleaned to manufacturer spec. Great prices without cutting corners on quality.

Alterations & Tailoring

Hems, tapers, resizing — we match the fit of garments you already love, so new clothes feel like they were made for you.

Steaming & Pressing

Need something ready fast? We steam and press garments — including costumes and formalwear — with quick turnaround times.

Laundry Service

Drop off your everyday laundry and let us handle the wash, dry, and fold — clean and ready when you are.

Formal & Special Occasion

Wedding gowns, quinceañera dresses, costumes — handled with the extra care that irreplaceable garments require.

Same-Day Turnaround

Urgent deadline? Call ahead and we'll do our best to accommodate — we've saved the day before and we'll do it again.
