The shop on the corner

A neighborhood cleaners that still does it by hand.

Mandalay Village Cleaners has looked after Port Hueneme's closets for years — the everyday button-down, the wool coat you can't replace, the dress you need taken in by Friday. Drop it off and it's handled by people, not a counter conveyor.

Tim and the team built their reputation on tailoring and repairs that actually fit: measured sleeves, resized jackets, fresh buttons, hems that hang right. Folks have been coming back for decades, and signing up for the after-hours bag-and-drop service so a busy week never gets in the way.
