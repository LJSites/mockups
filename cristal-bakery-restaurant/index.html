Our Story

Thirty Years of Real
Home Cooking

Cristal Bakery & Restaurant has been a fixture in Sanger for over three decades. The kitchen runs the way it always has — fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and the kind of care that makes every plate taste like something your grandma would have made. From a solo lunch to 100-burrito catering orders for a golf tournament, nothing is too big or too small.


The chimichanga is hand-made crunchy outside, soft and flavorful inside, served with house red sauce and a side salad. The rice is slow-cooked the right way. Come once and you'll understand why people have been coming back for 30 years.
