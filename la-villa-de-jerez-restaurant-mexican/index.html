Our Story

Relaxed Dining,
Real Flavors

La Villa de Jerez is Hollister's home for traditional Mexican cooking done right. From breakfast on weekends to hearty weekday lunches and dinners, every dish comes out of a kitchen that treats recipes like heirlooms — not shortcuts.

We're the kind of place you stumble onto, tell your friends about, then keep coming back to. Peppers roasted — not boiled. Menudo made to order on Sunday mornings. Salsas that earn their own reputation.

Find us on Google Maps — and call ahead on weekends.

What We Make

The Menu

01

Chiles Rellenos

Our most-talked-about dish. Peppers are hand-roasted over flame — never boiled — then stuffed and finished with care. The kind of chile relleno that makes people drive back to Hollister.

Fan Favorite
02

Enchiladas con Diabla

Cheese enchiladas bathed in our signature diabla sauce — a bold, smoky red that guests write home about. Order it once and it becomes your standing request.

Signature Sauce
03

Menudo

Slow-cooked, soul-warming, and ready Sunday mornings when you need it most. The place fills up by 11:30 AM — come early or call ahead.

Weekend Breakfast
04

Enchiladas de Chile Verde

Corn tortillas with tender pork in a tomatillo-verde sauce. Simple, satisfying, and the reason regulars never stray from the menu.
