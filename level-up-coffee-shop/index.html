Our Story

More than coffee —
a place that cares.

Level UP Coffee Shop on North Brand Blvd in Glendale is where quality meets genuine hospitality. We craft specialty coffees, pile fresh ingredients into every sandwich, and make sure every order — whether it's a solo cup or a team catering spread — is beautifully packed and full of flavor.

Our outside tables catch the breeze on good weather days. Our team knows your order. And every detail, from the cut fruit cups to the fresh-baked cake, is made with care you can taste.
