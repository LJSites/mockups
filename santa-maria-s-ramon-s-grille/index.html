Our Story

Where BBQ Smoke Meets Mexican Heart

At Santa Maria's Ramon's Grille, we do things our way: slow-smoked Santa Maria–style tri-tip, scratch-made Mexican staples, and a whole lot of heart — all served out of a cozy spot on Red Bluff's Main Street.

Whether you're swinging by for a quick lunch or feeding the whole crew, you'll leave full, satisfied, and already planning your next visit. The rodeo fries have their own fan club, and the birria tacos earn regulars for life.
