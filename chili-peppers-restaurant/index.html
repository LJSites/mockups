Our Story

A little tucked away,
worth the find.

For more than three decades, Chili Peppers has been quietly serving some of the most flavorful Mexican food in San Luis Obispo — a place regulars call a hidden gem and first-timers never forget.

Walk past the bright yellow facade on Broad Street and you'll find a funky, hand-painted Aztec garden: live plants, toucans on the wall, a stone fountain, and a big covered patio made for long lunches and easy dinners. The chips are homemade, the salsas keep coming, and the staff treats you like a regular from the first visit.
