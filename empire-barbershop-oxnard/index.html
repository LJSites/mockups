The Shop

Built on the Craft

Step through the doors at D101 and you're greeted by name. Empire Barbershop runs on a simple standard: take your time, sweat the details, and send every client out the door sharper than they walked in.

Our chairs are staffed by barbers who treat the work as a craft, not a conveyor belt — Bryan's million-dollar fades and scissor work, Angel's quiet consistency, Isaac's clean blowouts and tapers. Bring the kids; they're regulars here too.

The room is bright, the welcome is genuine, and the result speaks for itself. Whether it's a sharp taper, a full beard sculpt, or a custom design, you'll leave looking the part.
