About Us

Your Brawley
Tire Experts

Falcon Tires Inc. is a trusted full-service tire and auto repair shop serving the Brawley community. We specialize in tires, brakes, and a full range of vehicle maintenance — all with honest estimates that are clear and easy to understand.

Our friendly team, led by Alex and the front desk crew, makes sure you're comfortable and well-informed while we work on your vehicle. We built our reputation on being reliable and fair — and we intend to keep it that way.

We proudly serve individual customers, families, and repeat clients who trust us with every vehicle they own.
