Our Story

A neighborhood spot you can trust with your best clothes.

Mimo Alterations & Cleaners has been a trusted part of the Fremont community, tucked right on Fremont Boulevard. Teresa runs a tight, friendly operation — small enough to know your name, experienced enough to handle anything from a simple hem to a full dry-cleaning run.

We believe great tailoring shouldn't cost a fortune. Whether you need pants shortened, a dress taken in, or your best suit dry cleaned before an important day, Mimo delivers quality work with care and speed you'll notice.

What We Do

Alterations & cleaning
done right, every time.

From quick hems to professional dry cleaning, Mimo offers a full range of garment services at fair, transparent prices.

01

Hemming & Length Alterations

Pants, skirts, dresses — precise hemming so your clothes fit exactly the way they should. Clean finishes every time.

02

Dry Cleaning

Professional dry cleaning for suits, dresses, and delicate fabrics. Prompt turnaround, excellent results.

03

Tailored Fits

Waist-ins, side seams, taper — we reshape garments so they move with you, not against you.

04

Zipper & Repair Work

Broken zipper, split seam, loose buttons — fast, affordable repairs that extend the life of your favorite pieces.

05

Wedding & Formal Wear

Gowns, suits, bridesmaid dresses — careful handling and skilled alterations for your most important occasions.

06

Pressing & Finishing

Steam pressing and professional finishing so your clothes look freshly bought, every single time.
