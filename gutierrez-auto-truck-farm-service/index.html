About the shop

One yard for everything that rolls.

Gutierrez Auto Truck & Farm Service sits on East Paige Avenue in Tulare — Central Valley ag country, where a flat tire can mean a field that doesn't get worked. We grew up around trucks and tractors, and we built this shop to keep all of it moving.

Walk in with a passenger car, a commercial big rig, or a piece of farm equipment and you'll get the same thing: technicians who actually know the mechanics, a wall of name brands — Yokohama, Firestone, Bridgestone — and a price you can live with.

No appointment circus, no run-around. Pull in, tell us what's wrong, and we'll have you back on the road or back in the field as fast as it can be done right.
