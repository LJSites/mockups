Our Story

A HIDDEN GEM IN THE DESERT

D' Coffee Bouteaque is more than a café — it's a thoughtfully designed space where every detail has been crafted with heart. Husband-and-wife owned and operated, the Bouteaque brings an unmatched blend of stylish ambiance, extensive menus, and genuine warmth to Cook Street in Palm Desert.

From flat whites and Mexican hot chocolate to Fantasy salads and eggs Benedict, every plate and cup is treated as a work of art. Indoor and outdoor seating, a full breakfast-through-dinner menu, and a drink list deep enough to pull you back weekly.
