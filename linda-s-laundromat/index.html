About Us

Where clean is more than a word.

Linda's Laundromat has served the Salinas community at 1942 N Main Street with a simple promise: a facility you'd actually want to spend time in. Floors scrubbed, machines wiped down, surfaces that look like they were cleaned this morning — because they were.

We've invested in a full lineup of modern machines — small, medium, large, and super-large — all equipped with extra rinse cycles so allergens and detergent residue get left behind, not on your clothes.

Our laundry card system takes cash and credit cards, so you're never scrambling for quarters. Just load up and go.
