The Castro way

A garage you can actually trust

Castro Auto Repair Service is a working, family-run shop on Garvey Ave — the kind of place where the owner, Juan, still does the diagnosis himself and figures out the problems other mechanics walk away from.

We keep it simple: tell you what's wrong, show you the parts coming off and going on, quote a fair price up front, and get you back on the road. No upselling, no scare tactics — just careful work and a handshake.

“He'll show you the part, explain it, and never try to scam you.”
