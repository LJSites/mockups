Our Story

A Family Tradition, One Cake at a Time

For more than 25 years, J & J Bakery Cookies Bouquet has been a beloved destination for families across Southern California. People drive from Moreno Valley, Riverside, and beyond for one simple reason — our cakes are worth it.

We specialize in fresh fruit cakes layered over a signature crunchy pastry base, custom occasion cakes, festive piñatas, and small pastries for everyday indulgence. Every order is made with care by a team that genuinely loves what they do.

The owner is a sweetheart — approachable, attentive, and proud of every single cake that leaves this shop. Come say hi.
