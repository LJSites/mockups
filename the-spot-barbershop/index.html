The Shop

Your spot on Foothill.

Some folks have been coming since they were kids. Others walked in five years ago and never looked for another barber again.

The Spot is a working Fontana barbershop — a real chair, a real crew, and a cut that's on point whether you book a regular or walk in off Foothill Blvd. The room moves fast and friendly: you get greeted, you get sat, and you walk out fresh.

Fades, tapers, line-ups, beard sculpting, and patient kids' cuts — the barbers here take pride in getting you exactly what you asked for, and aren't afraid to try something new when you want to switch it up.
