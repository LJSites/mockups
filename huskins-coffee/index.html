Our Story

Small-Batch Roasting. Big-Hearted Hospitality.

Huskins Coffee is a specialty roaster and café tucked into the corner of 1st Street in Santa Ana. We roast in-house — you might catch Dev mid-batch when you walk in — and every cup is built on clean, complex flavors you can actually taste.

With Canadian roots, we brought real maple syrup north of the border: our Hometown Latte is the proof. Pair it with avocado toast or a freshly-baked oatmeal cookie, find a window seat, and stay a while.

Beyond the coffee we pour beers on tap, keep a light lunch menu, and make sure the Wi-Fi is solid — because the best meetings and deep-work sessions belong here.
