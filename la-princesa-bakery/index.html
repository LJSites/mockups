Our Story

A Gem in
Cudahy

La Princesa Bakery has been a beloved neighborhood institution for generations. Walk in and you'll find regulars who have been coming for over 20 years — and a staff who greets them by name.

From perfectly fluffy conchas to elaborate wedding cakes with coffee mousse and strawberry crème, every item is made with care. We specialize in custom cakes for any occasion — baby showers, birthdays, quinceañeras, and weddings — as well as fresh tamales, chocoflan, churro cheesecake, and rotating seasonal treats.

Come in for a concha and a coffee. Stay because it feels like home.
