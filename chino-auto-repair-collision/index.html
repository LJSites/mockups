Who We Are

A Shop That Treats Your Car Like Its Own

Run by owner Sarkis and a crew that has kept Chino moving for years, Chino Auto Repair & Collision pairs old-school honesty with the precision a modern vehicle needs — from a fender-bender on a daily driver to frame-true collision work on a sports car.

You get a clear estimate, original-spec parts, and a straight answer every time. No upsell, no guesswork.
