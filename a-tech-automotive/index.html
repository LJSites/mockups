Who We Are

Family Shop,
Serious Skills

Owner Patrick and his wife Patty built A Tech Automotive on a simple premise: treat every customer's car like it's your own. From a 2004 Highlander with 150k miles to motorcycles and diesel trucks — if it rolls in, they fix it.
