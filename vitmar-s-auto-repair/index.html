The shop

Honest work, done quick.

Vitmar's is the kind of neighborhood garage Salinas drivers keep coming back to. Whether it's a check-engine light after work, brakes that need replacing before a long drive, or a tune-up to keep an older car running strong — the crew gets to the diagnosis fast and tells you straight what it needs.

Need a tow? We handle towing and the repair together, so you're not bouncing between companies during rush hour. And because the whole team works in both English and Spanish, nothing gets lost in translation — you'll always know exactly what's happening with your car.
