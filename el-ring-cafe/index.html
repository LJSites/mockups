Our Story

A New Kind of Gathering Place in DTSA

El Ring Cafe arrived in Downtown Santa Ana as something genuinely different — a coffee house with a culinary identity. The espresso program is thoughtful, the latte menu leans creative, and the kitchen goes well beyond a pastry case.

From the carne asada chilaquiles with a customizable toppings bar to the celebrated banana latte and honey strawberry matcha, every item is made with intention. The space itself invites you to stay a little longer.

Open Tuesday through Sunday, mornings only. Come early, order curiously.
