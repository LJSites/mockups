The Shop

A real barber shop on Main Street

Newby's Barber Shop is where Hesperia comes for a proper cut. No gimmicks, no reservation apps — just a full team of skilled barbers ready to take you in the moment you walk through the door.

From classic fades and tapers to full beard shaves, trims, and color, plus a licensed beautician on staff, Newby's offers more than most. Budget-friendly prices, walk-ins always welcomed, and a rotating crew of more than six barbers on busy days.

What We Offer

Full-service barbering & beauty

Walk in and choose the look you want. Every barber is ready to deliver — and if you find one you love, just ask for their schedule.

  1. Haircut Classic cuts, fades, tapers — executed clean. Walk right in. Walk-in welcome
  2. Beard Trim & Shape Edge work, line-ups, and full beard shaping to frame your face.
  3. Straight Razor Shave Traditional hot-towel shave — a ritual that never goes out of style.
  4. Hair Color Touch-ups, blends, and full color services for a refreshed look.
  5. Beauty Services Full-service beautician on staff. Just ask for her when you arrive. Ask at the desk
