Our Tea

A wall of jars, brewed by the cup.

Step inside and the first thing you notice is the shelf — dozens of glass jars of loose-leaf tea, hand-labeled, from red mountain blends to oolong. We brew fresh, shake to order, and let you decide the sweetness and the boba.

Regulars come for the Tiger Stripes milk tea, the strawberry cheesecake, and the Sunset. Some bring drawings to leave on the counter. It’s that kind of place — clean, calm, and easy to settle into for a while.
