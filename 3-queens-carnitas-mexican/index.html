Our Story

Three Queens,
One Kitchen

Tucked into Tres Pinos Road in Hollister, 3 Queens Carnitas is a neighborhood gem built on honest, flavorful cooking. Every order is fresh — from the slow-rendered carnitas to rice and beans made in-house daily.

We keep it simple and keep it real: quality ingredients, generous portions, and the kind of food that makes you repeat the same order every time because nothing needs to change.

And as a bonus — MMM Churros fries up fresh churros right in our space, dusted in sugar while you wait. Don't leave without one.
