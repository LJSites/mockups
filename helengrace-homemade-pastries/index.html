Our Story

A bakery born from the joy of baking

Jenny and the HelenGrace team bake everything from scratch — you can feel it the moment you walk through the door. The shop smells of warm pastry and fruit, and the owners greet you like a regular even on your first visit.

Weekly menus rotate to showcase seasonal produce and customer favorites. Pies, cakes, cookies, savory pot pies, and house-made preserves — every single thing made with an intention that shows up on your plate.
