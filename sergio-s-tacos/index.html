El Lugar

A neighborhood
grill, done right.

No frills, no shortcuts — just carne asada hitting the flat-top and a line that knows what it's here for.

Sergio's is a Commerce staple on South Atlantic for Mexican breakfasts, tacos and burritos — the kind of spot you swing by on the move, grab your food fast, and walk out happy. We run carne asada, al pastor, carnitas, lengua, cabeza, pollo, buche and tripas, with tortas, sopes, tamales and menudo every single day.

It's home-cooking food at a counter pace: fresh, moist, packed, and reliable every time. Wash it down with horchata made in house and you've got the whole point.
