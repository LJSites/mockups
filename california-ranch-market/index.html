Ranch Market.
Real Community.

California Ranch Market is the go-to stop on CA-74 in Menifee for families who want more than a grocery run. Walk in and you'll find fresh produce, Mexican pantry staples, specialty cuts, and a hot-meal counter turning out dishes you'd expect from your abuela's kitchen — not a chain.

Order at the hot meal counter, grab a ticket, and browse the store while it's being packed up. You pay everything at the register in one go. That's the ranch market way.
