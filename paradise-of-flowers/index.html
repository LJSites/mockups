01 — The Shop

The flower lady on
Oxnard Boulevard.

Paradise of Flowers is the kind of place a neighbor counts on — the rack out front stacked with fresh roses, carnations, and lilies, and someone behind the counter happy to put a bouquet together while you wait. Stop in on the way home from work and walk out with something beautiful.

From a single dozen roses to custom arrangements for birthdays, anniversaries, and every just-because in between, every piece is made fresh by hand. Open early, open late, and open every day of the week.
