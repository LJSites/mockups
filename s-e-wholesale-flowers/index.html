Who we are

A working flower market — not a fussy storefront.

We’re a wholesale flower supplier on E Central Ave, just off the freeway in San Bernardino. That means depth: coolers full of roses, carnations, snapdragons, stock, pampas and seasonal stems — sold by the bunch at prices that make sense whether you’re reselling, decorating an event, or grabbing flowers on the way home.

Need it built? Tell the crew “a bright bouquet” and they’ll put it together in about ten minutes — or call ahead and have it ready. From single bunches to wedding-scale ring arrangements and event work, S&E handles the small order and the big one the same way: with a smile, fair pricing, and flowers that actually last.
