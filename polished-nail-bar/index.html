Our story

A small salon
with a big heart.

From the moment you sit down, you're offered a coffee, water, or a glass of wine — and treated like family.

Since Annie opened the doors, Polished Nail Bar has built its name on detail-oriented work and genuine warmth. Annie and the team take their time: nails shaped perfectly, polish applied flawlessly, every set built to last.

The space is clean, bright, and relaxing, with top-quality products and a small boutique of lashes, lotions, and beauty finds up front. Whether it's a quick fill or a full spa pedicure, you leave feeling cared for.
