The Spa

Where time slows
and hands are looked after.

Infinite Nails & Spa is a small, neighborhood nail studio on Long Beach Blvd. Our clients stay for years — some for two decades — because the work is careful and the room is calm. Clean, quiet, unhurried: the way nail care should feel.

Whether it's a simple mani-pedi to reset your week or detailed gel-X and nail art for something special, every set is shaped by hand and finished with patience. No conveyor belt, no rush — just good work and a little quiet.
