The Bar

Step in, and the noise of State Street falls away — marble, soft light, and the quiet hum of a room that takes its time.

Champagne Nail Bar is exactly that: a calming, intimate salon built around the details. Spotless from the floor to the chandeliers, with seasoned technicians who treat tired hands gently and finish them beautifully.

We're known for our dip powder and our wide assortment of colors and styles — and for the small ritual of a glass of champagne or sparkling water the moment you sit down. It's a little touch that makes an ordinary errand feel like an afternoon off.
