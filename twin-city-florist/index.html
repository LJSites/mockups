About Us

THE HEART OF CALEXICO'S FLOWER SCENE

Twin City Florist has served the Imperial Valley for decades, earning the trust of families, event planners, and last-minute romantics alike. We pour genuine care into every stem — from a single rose to a full funeral tribute.

Maggie, Rosa, and our entire team treat every order as personal. You'll always reach someone who listens, helps you choose, and delivers flowers worthy of the person you're honoring.

Located at 309 W 2nd St in Calexico, we offer same-day delivery throughout the valley, custom event work, and the kind of attentiveness the big online companies simply can't match.
