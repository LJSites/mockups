Our Story

Where the Panadería Meets the Espresso Bar

Espressito is Waterford's own little jewel — a family-driven cafe that fuses the warmth of a Mexican bakery with the precision of a proper espresso bar. Every morning, pan dulce comes out of the oven with flavors you won't find anywhere else.

The space is airy, unhurried, and decorated with a vibrant mural that tells the story of the community. Whether you're stopping in before work or settling in for a slow Saturday, this is a place built for lingering.

The owners are the ones behind the counter — they'll walk you through every pastry, pull your shot perfectly, and send you off with something you'll think about all week.

What We Make

The Menu

Everything crafted in-house. Flavors rotate with the season — but a few staples never leave the case.

01

Espresso & Drip Coffee

Single-origin shots, lattes, cortados, and drip — dialed in for clarity, not just heat. The straight espresso is the move.

02

Specialty Lattes

Horchata latte (creamy, cinnamon-forward), seasonal spiced drinks, and house originals. Ask about the sweetness level — they'll adjust.

03

Matcha & Non-Coffee Drinks

A rotating lineup of matcha preparations and refreshers — clean, balanced flavors made for those skipping espresso today.

04

Pan Dulce & Conchas

Baked fresh each morning in flavors like horchata, pumpkin spice, and Biscoff. Pillowy, distinct, and a reason to come early before they sell out.

05

Breakfast Sandwiches

Egg and cheese on fresh bread — simple, well-executed, satisfying. A perfect companion to your morning espresso.

06

Seasonal Specials

The owners regularly introduce limited-run pastries and drinks tied to the season. Follow them or just come in and ask what's new today.
