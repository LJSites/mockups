Our Story

A Panadería
You Can Trust

Chela's Bakery #3 brings the warmth of a traditional Mexican panadería to Arlington Avenue in Riverside. We're the spot for families who know that a good pan dulce starts before sunrise — our bakers are in early every morning so the shelves are full of fresh bread when you walk in.

We keep things simple and honest: real ingredients, fair prices, and a clean, organized shop so every customer feels good about what they're bringing home. We carry pan dulce, flour tortillas, and serve champurrado — the good stuff your abuela made.

Open late every weekday until 8PM, and weekends until 7PM — because good pan shouldn't have a curfew.

What We Make

Fresh From
The Oven

Pan Dulce

Conchas, cuernos, polvorones, and more — a full rotating selection baked fresh each morning. Always soft, always worth it.

Tortillas

House-made flour tortillas, soft and pliable — perfect alongside any meal or straight off the comal. Corn tortillas vary by availability.

Champurrado

Thick, rich Mexican hot chocolate made in-house. The perfect companion to any pan dulce on a Riverside morning.

Specialty Breads

Seasonal and rotating specialty loaves — ask in-store what's fresh today. We always have something new coming out of the oven.
