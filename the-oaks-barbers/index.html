The Shop

A hidden gem
in Oroville

The Oaks Barbers isn't trying to be a chain. It's a neighborhood shop with one barber who genuinely cares about getting your cut right — the lengths, the taper, the finish.

Dana takes her time. Whether you're a first-timer or a regular, you leave feeling like the only client she had that day.

Walk-in welcome when there's room, or call ahead. Either way, expect precision and a clean result you'll actually be happy with.
