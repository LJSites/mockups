The Truck

A Turlock taco
town fell for.

When taco-truck quality around Turlock started slipping, David'z Tacos rolled in and reset the bar.

It's the whole family behind the window — the same crew that runs the Tres Jefe'z truck — turning out asada tacos, the famous burrito supreme, and loaded fries that regulars drive across town for. Covered outdoor seating, plenty of parking, and customer service people actually write home about.
