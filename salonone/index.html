Who we are

A salon built on listening — and results that last.

At SalonOne, every appointment starts with a real conversation. Our stylists — Michelle, Amanda, Patty, and the rest of the team — take the time to understand what you want and deliver results you'll actually love.

From same-day color corrections to precision cuts and everything in between, we keep the salon clean, the atmosphere warm, and the standards high. Located on Howard Road in Madera, we've become a go-to for clients who want more than a quick trim.

Walk-ins and appointments both welcome. Call us to get on the books.
