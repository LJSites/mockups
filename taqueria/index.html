The Spot

A real find on Stockdale Hwy.

Yes — it's inside a Chevron. That's part of the story. Behind the counter is a full taquería kitchen turning out quesabirria tacos with consommé, tamales, burritos and tortas, all made to order and handed over fast and hot. The dining area is clean, there are a couple of tables by the drive-thru, and on Friday through Sunday they fire up lunch out in the parking lot.

It's the kind of place you stumble onto, order on a hunch, and end up telling everyone about. The owner's usually the one at the register — polite, smiling, and ready to point you toward the birria.
