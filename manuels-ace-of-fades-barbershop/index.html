The Shop

More than a haircut

Tucked off Mercey Springs Road, Manuel's Ace of Fades is a neighborhood barbershop that takes the craft seriously. Every fade is dialed in, every line is clean, and every client leaves the chair looking their best.

Whether you're coming in for a classic taper, a skin fade, or a full beard shape-up, the crew here brings the same level of attention and care to every chair. Open seven days a week — because looking good doesn't wait.

Located at 1450 Mercey Springs Rd, Suite 101. Walk-ins welcome; call ahead for guaranteed slots during busy periods.
