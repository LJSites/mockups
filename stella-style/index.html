Our Story

A Patio Studio Like No Other

Stella Style is an intimate outdoor patio salon nestled in San Diego's North Park. The fresh breeze naturally carries away treatment fumes while the peaceful setting turns every appointment into a restorative escape.

Owner and master stylist Stella brings over 35 years of professional experience — trained in techniques found in Japan and Korea — to every client. Her philosophy is simple: understand the hair, educate the client, and deliver results that last.

Stella works with premium, formaldehyde-free keratin and straightening products. Every service includes a thorough consultation and a personalized aftercare walkthrough so your hair looks its best long after you leave.
