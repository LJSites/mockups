Our Story

A little corner spot with a big reputation.

Tucked into a historic storefront on Main Street, Kaloca's is the kind of neighborhood grill where the salsa is made fresh, the chips keep coming, and the staff remember your order. Locals will tell you straight: this is the place for Mexican food in Red Bluff.

From the carnitas to the chimichangas, everything comes out piping hot, packed with flavor, and plated like it means it — no wonder folks become regulars after a single visit.
