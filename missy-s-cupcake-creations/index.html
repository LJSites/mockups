Our Story

Baked Fresh,
Served with Heart

Missy's Cupcake Creations is a cozy, welcoming bakery in Ventura where everything is made from scratch. From beautifully decorated birthday cupcakes to flaky croissant sandwiches and fresh daily donuts — this is a neighborhood bakery that puts quality and care into every order. Pre-orders are accepted and ready on time, every time.


Find us at the Johnson Drive location Tuesday through Saturday. Stop in, grab a coffee, and discover why locals keep coming back.

What We Make

Fresh From the Kitchen

Custom Cupcakes

Beautifully decorated, homemade cupcakes for birthdays, celebrations, and everyday indulgence. Mini and standard sizes available. Pre-orders encouraged.

Fresh Donuts

Made daily in small batches — sweet, satisfying, and packed with flavor. Available while supplies last each morning.

Croissant Sandwiches

Flaky, buttery croissants filled with savory ingredients — a standout item that keeps customers raving. A must-try.

Pre-Orders & Special Events

Planning a birthday or event? Missy takes pre-orders and ensures everything is ready on time at a fair price. Call to arrange.

Coffee & Beverages

Pair your pastry with a fresh cup of coffee. A friendly, cozy experience every visit.

Surprise Bags

Available through 2g2g — a great-value mix of the day's baked goods. Grab one and discover what's fresh that morning.
