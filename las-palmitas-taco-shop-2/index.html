Our Kitchen

Slow food,
made with love

At Las Palmitas Taco Shop 2 we cook the unhurried way: nacho chips fried fresh to order, salsas built on the table-side stand, and every plate carried out to you when you dine in. Nothing sits under a lamp waiting.

That’s what regulars mean when they call us the best authentic tacos in El Centro. It isn’t fancy — it’s asada, birria, fish tacos and burritos done right, plus the carne asada fries people keep coming back for. Drive-thru when you’re short on time, a clean, easy dining room when you’re not.

Open seven days a week, 7 AM to 10 PM — breakfast burritos through a late dinner.
