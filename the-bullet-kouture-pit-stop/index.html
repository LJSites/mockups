Our Story

A Pit Stop Built on Patience & Care

The Bullet Kouture Pit Stop is Newman's go-to destination for pets who deserve the full treatment — deshedding, breed cuts, nail trims, and full-service grooming — delivered by groomers who genuinely love animals.

Lexie, Maudie, and the whole team are known for their calm, hands-on approach with anxious pets, rescues, and first-timers alike. Your dog doesn't need to be dreading their next appointment.

We take multiple pets at once, work around your schedule, and pour real artistry into every cut. Because your fur baby isn't just a client — they're family.

What We Do

Delivered with Care

Signature Service

Full Groom Package

Bath, blow-dry, breed-specific cut, ear cleaning, nail trim, and finishing spritz — the complete experience. Our groomers consult with you on the exact look and then trust their craft to deliver it perfectly.

Coat Care

Deshed Treatment

Deep conditioning deshed for double-coated breeds like German Shepherds, Huskies, and Labs. Dramatically reduces shedding and leaves the coat unbelievably soft.

Finishing Touch

Nail Trim

Hand-cut with clippers for dogs who need extra care. Our groomers are known for keeping even the most anxious pups calm — no muzzle required for most clients.

Bath Day

Bath & Brush

Shampoo, conditioning treatment, blow-dry, and thorough brush-out. Great for maintenance between full grooms or for dogs who just need a refresh.

Specialty

Anxiety-Friendly Grooming

We specialize in rescue dogs and pets with histories of fear or abuse. Slow, patient handling, no rush, no force. Many anxious clients leave calm and happy.

Add-On

Breed-Specific Styling

From Yorkies and Chorkies to Shepherds and beyond — describe your vision and let us execute. We love the creative side of grooming as much as the care side.
