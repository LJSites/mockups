Who we are

The shop that earns it

Lopez Auto Repair is a family-run garage — owned by Ben and his father — built on hard work, fair prices, and treating every customer straight.

From a 60,000-mile dealer-grade tune-up to grinding out a broken differential bolt that nobody else could touch, the work gets done right the first time. Drivers don't come here for the flash. They come because they trust the people under the hood.

Whether it's your daily commuter or a truck that has to make it across the country, you'll get an honest diagnosis, an honest price, and a car that runs better than when it came in.
