Our Story

Straightforward service you can trust.

JV Auto Repair is a locally owned shop on North Burke Street in Visalia. Jorge built this business on a single principle: tell people the truth about their vehicle, charge a fair price, and do the work right the first time.

Too many shops recommend unnecessary repairs — Jorge doesn't. He'll tell you what actually needs fixing now, what can wait, and what you can skip entirely. Customers come back because they trust the diagnosis, not just the price.

Whether it's a routine oil change or a complex engine repair, your car gets the same careful attention. Most repairs are done within a day.
