Our Story

A little corner cafe with a big heart.

Coffee & Desserts is a small, family-run spot on West Lincoln Avenue — the sort of place where the cashier remembers your order and the chairs are comfy enough to stay a while. Quiet, peaceful, and easy to love.

We pour hot and iced coffee, premium matcha, Thai tea, and slushes (add boba if you like), and plate up Korean comfort food — kimbap, teriyaki, spam musubi — alongside a case of fresh little pastries. Free wifi, indoor seating, storefront parking. Come study, catch up, or just take five.
