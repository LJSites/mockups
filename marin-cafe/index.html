Our Story

A neighborhood spot, run like family.

Marin Cafe is the kind of place where the owner remembers your order and the coffee is poured to your taste, not a chain's. It's small, it's warm, and it's grown a loyal following one friendly morning at a time.

Pull up a chair beneath the Paris and New York prints, order an Abuelita latte and a slice of strawberry cake, and stay a while. Breakfast is served until mid-day, the pastries are made fresh, and there's always a seat with your name on it.

Whether you're grabbing coffee on the way to work or settling in for a proper breakfast, you'll be greeted with a smile — every time.
