The Shop

The Bambino is a real Pomona barbershop — chairs, mirrors, and a chair full of regulars who keep coming back.

You'll find us right on W Holt Ave with the barber pole out front and the windows that read Walk-Ins Welcome. Inside it's busy, friendly, and dialed in: skin fades, clean tapers, crisp lineups, beard work, and cuts the kids actually sit still for.

There's a lounge with a TV and a Game Zone so nobody's bored waiting their turn — bring the boys, grab a seat, and walk out looking sharp.
