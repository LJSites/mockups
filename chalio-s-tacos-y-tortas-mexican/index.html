Our Story

A taquería worth the detour.

Tucked into the little town of Avenal just off the I-5, Chalio's is the kind of place road-trippers stumble onto once and then plan their drive around. The meat is cooked over a busy steam table, scooped to order, and piled onto a fresh tortilla — no shortcuts, no skimping.

Order by the taco, by the torta, or by the half-pound of carnitas, lengua and tripas crisped up the way you like. Whether you're running the Central Valley at 2 a.m. or cutting over toward SLO, the door's open and the salsa's ready.
