Our Story

A Name You Can Trust
in Pacific Beach

Daisy Cleaners and Alterations is a full-service dry cleaner, alterations studio, and embroidery shop serving the Pacific Beach neighborhood of San Diego. Owner Shah and the team bring attentive, personal service to every order — whether it's a wedding suit that needs a same-day rush or 62 embroidered uniforms for a local business.

Customers return time and again for the fair pricing, clear communication on timelines, and the kind of craftsmanship that makes a garment look and feel right. Walk-ins are welcome; rush orders are handled with care.

What We Do

Services

Call for Pricing
  1. 01 Dry Cleaning Professional cleaning for suits, dresses, coats, delicate fabrics, and specialty garments. Rush service available.
  2. 02 Alterations & Tailoring Hemming, taking in or letting out seams, adding belt holes, repairs, and custom fitting for any garment.
  3. 03 Embroidery Logo and name embroidery for uniforms, corporate apparel, and personal items — any quantity, completed on time.
  4. 04 Wash & Fold Laundry Drop off your laundry and pick it up clean, fresh, and neatly folded. Perfect for busy schedules.
  5. 05 Household Items Comforters, drapes, table linens, and other household items cleaned and returned in pristine condition.
