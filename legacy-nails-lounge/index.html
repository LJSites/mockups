Who We Are

Legacy Nails Lounge is a neighborhood nail salon on Long Beach Blvd in Lynwood — the kind of place where you can walk in without a week-long wait and walk out with nails that hold up. We focus on doing the work well and keeping it affordable.

Whether you want a clean, simple manicure or something more detailed, we take the time to get it right. No pressure, no hustle — just good nails at a fair price, Monday through Sunday.

What We Do

services offered
Manicure
Classic manicure with shaping, cuticle care, and your choice of polish — gel or regular. Clean lines, no shortcuts.
Pedicure
Soak, scrub, shape, and polish. We take the time to do a proper pedicure — feet that feel as good as they look.
Acrylic Nails
Full sets and fills using quality acrylic. Customizable in length and shape — almond, coffin, square, or natural.
Gel Extensions
Lightweight and durable gel extensions with a natural finish. Great for length without the bulk of traditional acrylics.
Nail Art
From subtle French tips to hand-drawn designs, nail art is available on any service. Ask us what's possible.
Dip Powder
Long-lasting dip powder manicures in a wide range of colors. No UV light required — cures strong and stays flexible.
