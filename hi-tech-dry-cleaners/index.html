Built for clothes you actually care about.

Hi Tech Dry Cleaners has been the go-to on Slauson Avenue for neighbors who want their garments back clean, pressed, and ready — fast. No tickets lost, no shrugged shoulders.

From everyday shirts to wedding gowns, formal wear, silk, wool, leather and suede, every piece is handled with the right process for the fabric. And because our tailoring is done on-site, a hem, a take-in, or a quick fix doesn’t mean another trip across town.

The result is the kind of service regulars come back for: welcoming people, fair pricing, and clothes that come back better than you left them.
