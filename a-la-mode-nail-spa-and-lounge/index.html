The Salon

A stunning, elegant space — and the kind of welcome that brings families back every few weeks.

A La Mode is a nail spa and lounge built around the feeling of being properly looked after. The room is calm and immaculate, the color wall runs deep, and the team — led by Christina, David, and their crew — greets you at the door and pampers you through every minute of your visit.

Come in for a quick polish change or settle into a deluxe pedicure and a full set. Either way, you walk out happy. That's the whole idea.
