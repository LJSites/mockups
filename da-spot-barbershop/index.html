The Spot

Da Spot is a neighborhood barbershop on Philadelphia Street in Pomona — the kind of chair you keep going back to.

The crew here is known for a steady, gentle hand and cuts that come out clean every visit. Regulars talk about walking in and coming out feeling like a different person — and bringing the kids along, too.

Cuts run by appointment, so give a call ahead and we'll save you a spot. No rush in the chair, no guesswork on the fade.
