The Shop

Desert Automotive Services / Desert RV Service keeps the Imperial Valley moving — from daily drivers and work trucks to the RVs that get families down the road. We're a small, hands-on crew on Main Street in Brawley, and we treat every job like it's our own vehicle on the lift.

What customers tell us they appreciate most is plain talk: you'll meet the people doing the work, get a ball-park number up front, and hear an honest timeline before anything starts. No upsell theater — just the repairs your vehicle actually needs.

Auto or RV, we'd rather earn the next visit than rush this one.
