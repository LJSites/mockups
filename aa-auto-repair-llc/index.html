The Shop

Diagnosed Straight.
Fixed Right.

AA Auto Repair is a Tulare shop where the work gets explained before it gets done. Bring in a car with a check-engine light or a stubborn transmission, and Daniel walks you through what's wrong, what it'll take, and what it'll cost — often with photos sent straight to your phone — before any wrench turns.

What sets the shop apart in the valley: it doesn't stop at passenger vehicles. Daniel and the crew handle diesel work, heavy and industrial equipment — Caterpillar, John Deere, Bobcat — and the trailers that haul it all. One yard, one mechanic you can trust, whether it's a sedan or a skid steer.

Transparent pricing. No guesswork. Equipment that leaves running like new.
