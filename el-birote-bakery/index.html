Our Story

A neighborhood bakery
worth the drive.

El Birote Bakery has been a cornerstone of Porterville's community for years — a place where the bread is always soft, always fresh, and always made the right way. Our bakers arrive early so your first stop of the day is the best one.

We take pride in every pan dulce, every crusty birote roll, every buttery conchas and rich cheesecake. It's the kind of baking that turns first-time visitors into monthly regulars — some coming from as far as Bakersfield just for a taste of home.

Open every day 6 AM – 9 PM — because fresh bread shouldn't wait for business hours.

What We Make

From Our Oven to Your Table

Made fresh each morning — come early for the best selection, or call ahead to reserve a favorite.

Pan Dulce

Conchas, polvorones, empanadas, cuernos — our sweet breads are baked in batches all day so they never sit long on the shelf.

Birote Rolls

The namesake. Crispy outside, soft and chewy inside — the classic Mexican bolillo elevated with our own recipe developed over years of practice.

Fresh Loaves

Soft sandwich bread, telera, bolillo — baked every morning so every loaf you take home is genuinely, undeniably fresh.

Cheesecake & Pastries

Rich, creamy cheesecake at a price that surprises people. Customers say "jaw-dropping" — we just call it Tuesday.
