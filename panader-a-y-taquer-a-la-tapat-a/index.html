A real bakery on 11th Ave

We’re a family panadería and taquería in the heart of Hanford. The ovens are already going before the sun is up, so by the time the door opens the cases are stacked with conchas, orejas, cuernitos and whatever the bakers felt like making that morning.

“I love how the store smells early in the morning.”

Alongside the bread there’s tres leches and custom cakes, gelatinas, arroz con leche, japanese peanuts, and warm tamales by the dozen. Everything fresh, everything from scratch — the way a neighborhood bakery should be.
