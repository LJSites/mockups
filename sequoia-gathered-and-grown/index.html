Our Story

Rooted in the
Central Valley

Sequoia Gathered and Grown is a locally-owned floral studio tucked in Exeter, California — a small town surrounded by orchards and open sky. We believe flowers should feel as natural as the land they come from.

Every arrangement is hand-crafted with fresh, carefully selected blooms. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, honoring a loved one, or just because — we put real care into every stem.

We're open Thursday through Saturday, so come by and see what's in season. We'd love to help you find something beautiful.

What We Do

Arrangements for
every occasion

We offer hand-crafted floral for life's meaningful moments. Visit us Thursday–Saturday to browse in-shop arrangements or inquire about a custom order.

Fresh Bouquets
Seasonal, hand-gathered arrangements ready for gifting — birthdays, holidays, and everything in between. Available in-shop Thursday through Saturday.
Custom Arrangements
Have a specific vision or color palette in mind? We'll craft a one-of-a-kind piece built around your occasion and style preferences.
Gift Floral
Surprise someone with a beautifully wrapped bouquet or vase arrangement — our gift florals are designed to make a lasting impression on the recipient.
Seasonal Specials
Mother's Day, Valentine's Day, and other seasonal occasions call for something extra special. We curate limited collections that reflect the spirit of each season.
Special Events
Planning a small gathering or intimate celebration? Ask us about event floral — we'll work with you to find the right look for your space and budget.
