Full-Service Auto Care

Engine & Diagnostics

Full diagnostic scans and engine repair. We find the root cause — not just the symptom — before we touch anything.

02

Cooling System

Radiator leaks, coolant flushes, thermostat and hose replacement. We got a 2020 Ram 1500 back on the road in two days.

03

Seals & Gaskets

Rear main seal, valve cover, head gasket. Precise teardown and reassembly so the repair holds the first time.
