Who We Are

A garage that actually cares about you

John's Garage has been serving the Yucca Valley community from our spot on Old Woman Springs Road. We built this place on one simple belief: auto service should be straightforward, affordable, and kind — every single time.

What We Do

Services & Specialties

New tires sourced and installed while you wait. We carry a range of sizes for passenger cars, vans, and trucks — with competitive pricing and fast mounting. Need a patch or plug? We handle those same day, often in under 30 minutes.

From brake jobs to fuel pump replacements, our mechanics tackle the full range of repairs. We use quality parts and quote you honestly before we wrench — no surprise invoices.

Caught a screw in your tire on the way through 29 Palms or heading up to Big Bear? We get road travelers in fast. Quick diagnosis, honest repair vs. replace recommendation, and you're back on the road the same visit.

Desert roads are tough on brakes. We inspect pads, rotors, calipers, and brake fluid as part of every visit — and let you know what's actually needed, not what runs up the bill.

Routine oil changes, filter replacements, and fluid top-offs. Keep your vehicle dialed in between bigger services — we'll remind you what's coming due and keep things simple.
