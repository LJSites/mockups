About Us

YOUR FULL-SERVICE BEAUTY DESTINATION

Diamond Nails & Lashes is Bakersfield's go-to nail and lash studio — a full-service salon where skilled technicians take time to listen, so every set reflects exactly what you envisioned.

From next-level massage chairs and jelly pedicures to precision acrylic fills and lash extensions, every service is delivered with care, patience, and real technique. Walk-ins welcome; appointments appreciated.

We're open seven days a week on Stockdale Hwy. Cash is preferred — an ATM is on-site — but we also accept card, Apple Pay, and tap.
