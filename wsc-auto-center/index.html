Who We Are

Your trusted shop in the Inland Empire.

At WSC Auto Center, we believe that finding a mechanic you can genuinely trust shouldn't be hard. Under the care of Manager Cao Wang, our team brings consistent honesty, fair pricing, and thorough work to every vehicle that comes through our doors.

Whether you're bringing in a high-end European import, a daily commuter, or a supercar that needs custom fitment work, we treat every job with the same attention to detail and transparency. That reputation has made us a go-to shop for Chino and the surrounding Inland Empire.

Our customer lounge offers wifi and a comfortable rest area — because your time matters too.
