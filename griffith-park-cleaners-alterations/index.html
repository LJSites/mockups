Our Story

A neighborhood institution since day one

Griffith Park Cleaners & Alterations has been a fixture on Hyperion Ave in Silverlake for years, earning a loyal following with consistent, careful work and genuinely warm service.

"When you walk in, you'll be greeted by Jack — a genuinely nice person, like everyone else working here."

Jack and the team combine state-of-the-art cleaning equipment with meticulous handwork. The in-house seamstress handles everything from a quick hem to full custom suit alterations — always at prices that won't surprise you.

Convenient parking right at the door, fast turnaround, and a staff that remembers your preferences: that's the Griffith Park difference.
