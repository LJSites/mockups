Our Story

Baked at home, finished by hand, given with love.

Hecho Con Amor is a small home bakery in Hanford where every cake starts as a conversation — your celebration, your colors, your favorite flavors. There’s no assembly line here: each cake is mixed, baked, and piped by hand.

From vintage heart cakes with delicate lambeth piping to filled cupcakes, red-velvet brownies, and playful custom designs, we love turning a simple idea into the centerpiece of your day. Birthdays, gender reveals, “just because” — if it’s worth celebrating, it’s worth a cake made with love.

— Hecho con amor, siempre.
