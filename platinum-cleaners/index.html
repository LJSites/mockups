The House of Platinum

A neighborhood cleaner with a couture conscience.

Some fabrics forgive. Most don't. We treat every piece as if its owner were watching — because, in a family business, they nearly always are.

Platinum Cleaners is a family-owned dry cleaner on North Brea Boulevard, where owner Mike has quietly built a reputation over twenty years for one thing: getting it right. Not the fastest, not the flashiest — the most careful.

We handle the everyday and the irreplaceable with the same patience: shirts and suits, ties and blouses, bedding and flags, and the delicate, machine-specific work of wedding gowns and graduation regalia that corporate chains turn away. Reasonable pricing, an honest turnaround, and a finish that looks new again.

What we tend to

Garment care, by hand and by craft.

01 Suits & Dress Shirts
Crisp pressing and dry cleaning for tailored suiting and shirting — funeral-ready or boardroom-ready, with a turnaround measured in hours, not days.
02 Wedding Gowns
The right machines and a steady hand. We lift yellowing and set-in stains from off-the-rack gowns and restore them bright — preservation available.
03 Gowns & Regalia
Graduation regalia, formal gowns, and delicate hoods steamed and pressed free of wrinkles — looking new again, at a reasonable per-piece price.
04 Blouses, Ties & Dresses
Fine-fabric knowledge across the wardrobe — silks, linens, and structured pieces handled with the care each fiber asks for.
05 Bedding, Flags & Linens
Household textiles and large pieces — duvets, table linens, and flags — cleaned, pressed, and folded to order.
06 Difficult Fabrics
Brought us the piece another cleaner couldn't manage? That's the work we're known for. Ask Mike — twenty years of saying yes.

All work priced by the piece, fairly — bring your items in for an honest quote.
