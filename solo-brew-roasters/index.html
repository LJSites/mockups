A High-Desert Roastery

Solo Brew & Roasters is a craft coffee roaster and cafe tucked on Nomwaket Road in Apple Valley — one of the last places in the High Desert still roasting its own beans.

Every syrup and sauce is made in-house, which is why a single visit can turn into a standing order. The darker roasts lean earthy with dark-fruit notes and a clean acidity — bright, never burned. Like your coffee less sweet? Just ask — they'll happily dial it back.

It's run by two people building drinks one at a time, so weekend mornings draw a line. That line is the point: it's a small shop the community shows up for.

The Roastery

Beans, by the bag

We roast in small batches right here in Apple Valley. The result is coffee with character — earthy, dark-fruit-forward darker roasts with a clean, never-burned finish.

Take a bag home at a price that stays honest. Baked goods — including the famous vegan cinnamon roll — come out fresh and sell out fast, so the early crowd has the right idea.
