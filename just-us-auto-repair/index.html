The Shop

Just Us Auto Repair is exactly what it sounds like — just one trusted mechanic on West Prosperity Avenue, doing the work himself from start to finish. No layers, no upsell from someone who never touched your car.

People come here because they were sent by family, or because they got burned somewhere else and needed it fixed properly. He'll tell you straight what's wrong, get it handled quickly, and won't hand you excuses.
