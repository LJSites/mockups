Who we are

Two people who actually care for your clothes.

G & S Cleaners is a husband-and-wife shop in Paramount that treats every order like it's their own. Drop off a stack of dress shirts and pants, a leather jacket, a treasured baseball cap — they handle it with the same patience and skill.

Buttons that fall off get sewn back on. Zippers get changed. Complaints get made right. After more than a decade of regulars trusting them with twenty shirts at a time, that's just how it works around here.
