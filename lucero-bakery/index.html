Our Story

A family bakery, the way it should taste.

Lucero Bakery is a family-owned panadería on Gage Avenue in Commerce, turning out the Latin favorites our neighbors grew up on — and a few you'll drive across town for.

The stuffed churros are the thing people come back for: crisp, fresh, filled with cajeta, chocolate, guava or fresh strawberry. Alongside them you'll find tres leches in five flavors, gelatinas de leche, choco flan, conchas and a case full of pan dulce — plus warm champurrado and Mexican hot chocolate to go with it.

Doors open early — 5 AM most mornings — so the case is full before the rest of the block wakes up.
