Our Story

A flower shop with heart in San Bernardino

"They went above and beyond our expectations."

Bella's Flowers has been serving the Inland Empire community with artistry and genuine warmth. Whether you need a last-minute bouquet, hospital delivery, or full event florals, Gil and the team treat every order with the same care they'd give their own family's celebration.

Customers drive from across the valley — even from Redlands — because the quality and service are worth the trip. Seven days a week, 9 to 5, we're here.
