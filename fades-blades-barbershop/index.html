The Shop

A Gem on Lincoln Boulevard

Fades & Blades isn't a chain. It's a real barbershop — the kind that's always full of clients because the work speaks for itself. Owner and barber Mike Webb has been taking care of Oroville for years, and every barber on the floor brings that same standard.

Walk in, wait your turn, leave looking sharp. Cash only — no frills, no nonsense, just honest craft and the best haircut in Northern California.
