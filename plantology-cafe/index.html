Our Story

More than a cup — a whole world to explore.

Plantology Cafe sits just off South Coast Highway in Oceanside, a rare place where specialty coffee and living botanicals share the same sunlit space. We source our coffee from local vendors who share our values, and we've built our menu around flavors that surprise you.

Every corner celebrates Latina, women, and LGBT empowerment — because a cafe should reflect the community it calls home. Pick up a plant, pot your own in our potting room, or simply linger over a café de olla.

The Plant Side

A nursery inside a cafe. Seriously.

Browse carnivorous plants, rare tropicals, and varieties you won't find at any garden center — all at surprisingly reasonable prices. Take one home, or stay and pot your own in our dedicated potting room.

It's the kind of place that makes you slow down, look closer, and leave with something green tucked under your arm.
