A shop the valley trusts

At Brawley Auto Service & Repairs, the work starts with a real diagnosis — not a guess. Javier and his team check the vehicle, tell you exactly what's wrong, and walk you through the pricing before they begin. Then they get it done with a turnaround that keeps you on the road.

From a noisy AC compressor in the Imperial Valley heat to worn brakes, tired suspension, or a full engine or transmission replacement, this is a shop that handles the whole car. Customers come back because the answers are honest and the work holds up.

It's the kind of place that sends you photos of the problem so you understand what you're paying for — and treats a stranded traveler the same way it treats a regular.
