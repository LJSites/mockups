Our story

A boutique experience,
not a factory visit.

Boutique Nails and Spa was built around a simple belief: nail care should feel personal. Walk in and you're greeted by name. Sit down and the massage chair starts before you've even chosen a colour. There's no clock on the wall pushing you out the door.

Our technicians — Holly and Tony — have spent years refining their craft, and they still take the time to get every detail right, every single visit. That's not a policy; it's how they work.
